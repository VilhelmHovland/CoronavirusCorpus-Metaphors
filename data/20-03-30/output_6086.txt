Health workers start tracing close contact of Vizcaya COVID-19 victim  BAYOMBONG , Nueva Vizcaya , March 27 ( PIA ) -- Health workers here have started the contact tracing procedures for the first COVID-19 fatality in Solano town , also the first fatality in the Cagayan Valley Region .
Edwin Galapon , provincial health officer said they have already dispatched their health workers to conduct the contact tracing for the primary , secondary persons and other individuals who might have experienced a close contact with the victim .
