Normally companies that are looking to merge have to notify FTC and DOJ ahead of time about mergers that reach certain thresholds .
The agencies have 30 days to review and authorize requests or ask for additional information that requires a second waiting period .
FTC and DOJ typically have the ability to end the initial waiting period early without a second request , but in light of coronavirus , early termination was halted .
Today 's announcement removes that safeguard and allows expedited mega-mergers to resume .
