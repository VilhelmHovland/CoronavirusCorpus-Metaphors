COVID-19 : Senators to donate 50 per cent of salaries  Senators have agreed to donate fifty per cent of their salaries , effective from this month as their financial contributions to combat the spread and treatment of COVID-19 in Nigeria .
Acting Chairman Senate Committee on Media and Public Affairs , Senator Godiya Akwashiki conveyed the position of senators in a statement on Monday .
Recall that the Ministers have made a similar commitment last week .
Senator Akwashiki said the lawmakers were willing to sustain the gesture until " Nigeria is declared safe from the ravages of this deadly disease .
"  The senator representing Nasarawa North appealed to Nigerians to adhere strictly to precautionary measures to combat the COVID-19 pandemic .
The statement read in part : " After due consultations following a keen review of the national efforts to contain the Coronavirus pandemic in Nigeria , the Senate wishes to announce that from March 2020 , Distinguished Senators will be donating 50 per cent of their salaries to these efforts to stop the spread of the disease , otherwise known as COVID-19 , in our country .
