MS Dhoni To Lead Wasim Jaffer 's All Time IPL XI  Former India opener Wasim Jaffer has picked his all-time IPL ? XI ? with MS Dhoni leading this side .
MS Dhoni has been one of the most successful captains in the history of the Indian Premier League as he has won 3 IPL trophies as a captain and played in 9 of the 12 finals so far .
Dhoni is the most successful captain in the IPL in terms of the number of matches won throughout the tournament .
Taking to Twitter , Jaffer posted his team in which Chris Gayle , Andre Russell , Rashid Khan and Lasith Malinga are the four foreign players in his 12-member team .
With all the cricketers being in isolation due to the outbreak of the Covid-19 virus , it has given them time to interact with their fans through social media .
Jaffer also conducted a Q&amp;A session on his Twitter account wherein he has made some interesting choices and revelations .
