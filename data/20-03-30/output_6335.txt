Japanese comedian Ken Shimura dies from coronavirus  TOKYO -- Japanese comedian Ken Shimura , who had been hospitalized after being infected with the new coronavirus , has died , public broadcaster NHK reported on Monday .
He was 70 .
A man wearing a protective face mask , following an outbreak of the coronavirus disease ( COVID-19 ) , walks past in front of a huge screen reporting death of Japanese comedian Ken Shimura , who had been hospitalised after being infected with the new coronavirus , in Osaka , western Japan March 30 , 2020 , in this photo taken by Kyodo .
Mandatory credit Kyodo/via REUTERS  Shimura , one of the country 's best-known comedians with a career dating back to the early 1970s , had been hospitalized in Tokyo and died on Sunday evening , NHK said .
