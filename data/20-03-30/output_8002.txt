courtesy of Max 's Deli in Highland Park  Daily Herald report  Updated 3/30/2020 4:55 PM  The sale of cookie doctors wearing masks have helped Joey Morelli of Max 's Deli in Highland Park serve more than 500 meals to the real doctors fighting coronavirus .
Morelli said the proceeds from the cookie sales go toward paying Morelli 's employees and the cost of the donated meals .
" People buy these cookies so I can keep cooking , " Morelli said .
" These hospitals are going to be busy the next six weeks , not the next six days , and I plan to keep participating the whole way through .
