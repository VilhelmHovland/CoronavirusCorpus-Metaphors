Warren Davidson Discusses Coronavirus Relief Package  Ohio Rep .
Warren Davidson , R-Troy , joins Cincinnati Edition to talk about the $2 .
trillion relief package to combat the spread of coronavirus and to help the economy , which was signed into law by President Trump last week .
The bill includes expanded unemployment insurance , tax rebates and loans to business , as well as additional support to fund the medical response to the coronavirus outbreak .
The bill also includes language that temporarily suspends federal foreclosures , evictions , student loans and provides relief to corporations and the airline industry .
