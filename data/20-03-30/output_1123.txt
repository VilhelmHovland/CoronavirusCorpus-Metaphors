Morrisons will give ? 10m worth of produce to food banks to help keep them stocked during the coronavirus pandemic .
The supermarket will also extend opening hours at its bakery , egg and fruit &amp; vegetale packing site by an hour day to boost food bank stocks .
Stores will also be sent produce including tinned food and pasta , so they can distribute it to local food banks , which have reported shortages as there is an increased demand for food as shoppers stock-up for their homes during the coronavirus lock down .
By July 2020 , Morrisons says ? 10m worth of food will be sent to food banks by the supermarket 's in-store community champions , who work with food banks in their area .
Typical items being donated include bread , eggs , orange juice and bags of pasta .
To make it easier for customers to donate to food banks , purchase limits will be raised raised from three items per customer to four , and some will be removed all together .
