IAF , USAF hold joint F-35 drill in southern Israel  Drill was given special permission to take place despite continued spread of coronavirus .
Despite the coronavirus , Israeli Air Force F-35s trained alongside American F-35s on Sunday in the Enduring Lightening training exercise .
