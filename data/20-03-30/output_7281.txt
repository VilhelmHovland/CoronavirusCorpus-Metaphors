Six new coronavirus cases reported in Georgia  30 Mar in 11:00  Georgia has reported six new cases of coronavirus earlier today , with the total number of confirmed cases standing at 97 .
According to Georgian health officials , it has been identified how the six new patients contracted the virus .
As of now there are 77 in the country infected with the virus ( two were foreign citizens who have already left the country via a charter flight ) , Agenda .
reported .
4,878 people in Georgia are under quarantine and 264 others in hospitals as of March 30 .
18 of the 97 have recovered .
Georgia reported its first case of coronavirus on February 26 , 2020 .
There have been no deaths related to the coronavirus in the country so far .
