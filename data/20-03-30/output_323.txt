Ceylon Petroleum Corporation ( CPC ) and Ceylon Petroleum Storage Terminals Limited ( CPSTL ) have joined efforts to contain the spread of COVID-19 in the country , by introducing ethanol-based hand sanitizer at all outlets .
The move was brought to the attention of Power and Energy Minister Mahinda Amaraweera during a visit to CPSTL today , to observe the distribution of fuel during the curfew period .
According to the Minister , one fuel station will remain open in every Police division , even when curfew is imposed , to ensure essential services continue without disruption .
Amaraweera also assured the public that CPC has an adequate store of fuel , and there will be no interruptions to fuel supply .
