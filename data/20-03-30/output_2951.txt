The PUNCH reports that Makinde is the third known governor that tested positive for the disease , after Bala Mohammed and Nasir El-Rufai of Bauchi and Kaduna States respectively .
Barely 24 hours before the governor tested positive , the Nigeria Centre for Disease Control announced four new confirmed cases in Oyo .
Among them are the Chief Medical Director , University College Hospital , Ibadan , Prof .
Jesse Otegbayo ; Provost and Deputy Provost of the College of Medicine , University of Ibadan , professors Ezekiel Olapade-Olaopa and Obafunke Denloye , respectively .
As of Sunday , Oyo State had recorded seven confirmed COVID-19 cases , according to the NCDC .
Earlier , the governor had imposed a dusk-to-dawn curfew in the state and suspended inter-state transportation into and out of the state .
