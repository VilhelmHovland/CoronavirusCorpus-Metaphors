FAIRMONT -- The City of Fairmont has made its own virtual COVID-19 " help desk , " hoping to connect residents and business owners with helpful information and resources .
City officials began working on the help desk last week , and are still in the process of adding updated information to the hub every day , according to Alex Petry , the city 's program manager of economic development .
" It 's a place where anyone can go to find resources regarding the coronavirus crisis , " Petry said .
" We have resources from the CDC and the Small Business Administration and FEMA .
We have state resources , healthcare resources and a link to all of the testing sites in West Virginia .
Also , any updates that the city has regarding the crisis will be posted there .
"  Petry also said unemployment information , updates from Gov .
Jim Justice , maps and more are available through the hub .
