To help the needful , Bollywood celebrities are doing their bit by donating to PM Cares Fund .
Joining the list are celebrities Kartik Aaryan , Anushka Sharma , Badshah and Bhumi Pednekar .
The pandemic coronavirus has been slowly and steadily spreading its wings in India .
As a precautionary measure , Prime Minister Narendra Modi has issued a 21-day lockdown in the country and has asked everyone to stay at home .
This lockdown has severely hit the daily wage workers in the country who are left with now work and no way to head back to their hometowns or villages .
The state and the central government is doing its best to ensure that the basic needs of these people are fulfilled .
Extending their support , several Bollywood celebrities are doing their bit by donating to the PM-Cares fund .
