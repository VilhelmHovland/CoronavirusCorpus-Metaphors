More states issuing stay-at-home orders starting Monday  By KCRG-TV9 News Staff&amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp  Posted : Mon 4:20 AM , Mar 30 , 2020&amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp  Updated : Mon 12:46 PM , Mar 30 , 2020  CEDAR RAPIDS , Iowa ( KCRG ) - Stay-at-home orders in 2 more states will go into effect Monday , bringing the total to 27 states .
At least 225 million people are currently under a stay at home order in the U .
Maryland , Kansas and North Carolina are the latest states to be added to the list .
Those orders go into place Monday .
In the Midwest , Minnesota , Wisconsin , Illinois , Indiana , Ohio and Michigan all have statewide stay at home orders .
At least a dozen other states have orders issued for different counties .
Kim Reynolds has not issued a stay-at-home order for Iowa .
Editor 's Note : An earlier version of this story showed Arkansas had stay-at-home orders starting Monday .
Arkansas has not issued a statewide stay-at-home order .
