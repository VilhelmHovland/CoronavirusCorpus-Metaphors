SAN ANTONIO -- United States Rep .
Will Hurd announced Monday that more than $19 million in resources will be distributed to San Antonio , El Paso and Bexar County to address the COVID-19 public health emergency .
The resources will be distributed through programs administered by the U .
Department of Housing and Urban Development .
" This dangerous virus affects all Americans regardless of their background or socioeconomic status .
The more than $19 million in resources will allow our localities to continue combating the outbreak among some of our most vulnerable populations , " Hurd said in a statement .
The announcement comes after the CARES Act passed last week .
