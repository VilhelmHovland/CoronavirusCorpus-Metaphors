Uttar Pradesh : Five of family test positive for COVID-19 in Meerut ; state count rises to 55  A man , his wife , and three brothers-in-law have been found infected with the viral disease in UP 's Meerut .
Representational Image&amp;nbsp  Meerut : Five people of a family have been tested positive for coronavirus in Meerut , taking the total number of COVID-19 cases in Uttar Pradesh to 55 on Saturday , an official said .
A man , his wife , and three brothers-in-law have been found infected with the viral disease in UP 's Meerut and they have been shifted to a quarantine facility in the state .
" First a man was tested positive for coronavirus .
Later , his wife and his three brothers-in-law were also found to be positive .
They have been put into quarantine facility , " news agency ANI quoted Dr Rajkumar , Chief Medical Officer ( CMO ) CCL Hospital , as saying .
The man , who was tested COVID-19 positive first had visited three houses and had come in contact with around 50 people in the area , according to @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ The man had visited three houses and came in contact with 50 people .
The reports of these 50 people are yet to come .
After which we will know if there is any surge in the cases , " Dr Rajkumar said .
According to the State government , eight laboratories have been set up for testing - three in Lucknow and one each in Aligarh , Varanasi , Meerut , Etawah , and Gorakhpur , while one lab will be set up in Jhansi .
A total of 2,196 samples have been tested in the state so far .
" Efforts are being made to take this count to 15,000 in the public sector .
Talks are also on with private hospitals in several districts of the state , " Medical and Health Principal Secretary Amit Mohan Prasad said .
