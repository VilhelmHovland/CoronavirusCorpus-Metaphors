Sheboygan County : 10 coronavirus cases , 4 active , 6 recovered  SHEBOYGAN - Sheboygan County announced its 10th confirmed case of COVID-19 Monday .
The county has four active cases -- all are at home in isolation .
The other six patients have recovered .
The people in the county who have gotten COVID-19 have contracted it through international travel , contact with existing cases and community spread , according @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ Sheboygan County health officials have worked with known cases to track down and inform anyone who may have had contact with them .
Common symptoms of COVID-19 include fever , cough and shortness of breath .
If you think you have symptoms , call your doctor or the emergency room before going in for testing .
If you have questions or immediate needs related to COVID-19 , you can text COVID19 to 211-211 , visit 211Wisconsin .
or call 211 .
Testing supplies are limited , and some people with milder symptoms have been told to stay home and quarantine themselves rather than seek testing and treatment at a hospital .
Drive-through testing started the week of March 23 at Ascension and Prevea in Sheboygan .
It is not open to the public and still requires an order from a doctor .

All people are being advised to wash their hands and disinfect surfaces regularly .
The elderly and people with pre-existing conditions that could make them vulnerable to the coronavirus are advised to stay home as much as possible .
