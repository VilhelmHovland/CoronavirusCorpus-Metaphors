Humana to waive coronavirus-related treatment costs for customers  LOUISVILLE , Ky .
-- Health insurance giant Humana is stepping up to provide relief for those seeking treatment related to the COVID-19 pandemic .
The Louisville-based company announced Monday morning that it is waiving costs for treatment related to coronavirus-covered services .
This builds on Humana 's previous pledge to cover out-of-pocket costs for coronavirus testing .
