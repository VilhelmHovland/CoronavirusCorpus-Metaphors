The decision to postpone the annual general meeting until June 23 comes after France prolonged its official confinement until mid-April .
PARIS -- Add Kering to the growing list of companies that have postponed their annual general meeting due to the coronavirus crisis .
At a meeting on Monday , Kering 's board decided to postpone its AGM to June 23 from April 23 , the luxury group said in a statement .
France on Friday extended its official confinement period until April 15 , although it could be prolonged until the end of the month .
" A press release will be issued later to inform shareholders of the arrangements for the general meeting , the payment of the dividend as well as other resolutions submitted to their approval , " Kering said .
The French government has asked companies who have benefited from emergency government aid , including state-backed loans and a postponement of taxes and social charges , to refrain from paying a dividend this year .
