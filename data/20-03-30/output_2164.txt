Kirsty Wigglesworth/The Associated Press  The British government was hit with another coronavirus infection Monday just as Prince Charles ended his self-isolation after testing positive for the virus last week .
The Prince of Wales " is in good health and is now operating under the current standard government and medical restrictions that apply nationwide , " said a statement released Monday from Clarence House , his London residence .
Just hours later , government officials confirmed that Prime Minister Boris Johnson 's key adviser , Dominic Cummings , had developed COVID-19 symptoms over the weekend and was self-isolating at home in London .
Cummings has been a lead architect of the government 's agenda , particularly on Brexit .
