Health First has plans in place that could add 276 beds to that count , 87 of them ICU beds .
" We are working closely with Brevard County as well as local hotels on how we could partner in the event of over-capacity and being able to use their resources to care for those who are sick , " Johnson wrote , noting that the preparations are no different than those being undertaken by health systems nationwide .
The newly released details followed a FLORIDA TODAY article published last week in which Health First refused to release exact figures for its ICU capacity or ventilators .

Brevard County 's Emergency Operations Center on Monday moved to a " Level 1 " emergency activation in response to the COVID-19 pandemic , which allows them to mobilize all emergency management personnel along with county departments , municipalities and nonprofit organizations .
The county plans to take several actions to address the pandemic in Brevard , including surveying hospitals about ventilator and anesthesia machine inventory and current usage rate .
Beyond that , the county will plan for potential isolation and quarantine centers and field hospitals , purchase additional protective equipment for health workers , urge the state to implement a drive-through testing site and implement a strategy to supplement existing food banks , which are seeing an increase in need from the community .
