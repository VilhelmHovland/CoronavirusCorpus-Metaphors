PTI photo  New Delhi : The Supreme Court on Monday sought a status report from the Centre by Tuesday on the measures taken in view of the large-scale migration of labourers from cities to their native villages amid the coronavirus outbreak and the consequent lockdown across the country .
The top court observed that the migration of labourers out of panic and fear is becoming a bigger problem than the coronavirus .
It said the court would not create more confusion by issuing directions on measures that the government is already taking to deal with the issue .
