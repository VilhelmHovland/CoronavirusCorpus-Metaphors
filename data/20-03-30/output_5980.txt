Woman dies of coronavirus in Mumbai : Maha toll reaches 7  Posted by Qayam Published : March 29 , 2020 , 1:37 pm IST  Mumbai : The test reports of a 40-year-old woman who died in Mumbai on Saturday have come out positive for coronavirus , taking the death toll of COVID-19 patients in Maharashtra to seven , a civic official said on Sunday .
The woman was admitted to a civic hospital here on Saturday after she complained of severe respiratory distress , the official said .
" She died on Saturday and her sample was sent for testing .
The report came out positive for coronavirus .
She is the seventh person who died of COVID-19 in the state , " the official said .
" The woman was complaining of breathlessness and chest pain since last three-four days .
She was also suffering from hypertension , " the official added .
