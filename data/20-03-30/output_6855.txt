CompuLynx makes available its ecommerce platform for free  Monday 30 March 2020 11:28 CET News  Software developer CompuLynx has invited retailers to sell products on its ecommerce platform free of charge for the next three months .
Retailers and businesses that had not previously adopted ecommerce were caught flat-footed by the disruption caused by Covid-19 .
According to CompuLynx , the move was prompted by the economic disruption caused by the Covid-19 pandemic which has seen businesses lose foot traffic due to a partial lockdown meant to stem the spread of the virus .
The online shopping platform will enable any retailer regardless of size to become an online store and continue providing convenient services to their consumers .
Customers can navigate to purchase products ranging from groceries , beverages , snacks , personal care and baby items , home appliances , among others .
