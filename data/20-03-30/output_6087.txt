Caloocan LGU ensures safety of street dwellers against COVID  CALOOCAN CITY , March 29 ( PIA ) -- The Caloocan City Government , through its City Social Welfare and Development Office ( CSWD ) , has provided a temporary shelter for the city 's street dwellers at the University of Caloocan City both in the Biglang Awa and Camarin .
Included in the shelter are provisions of food , sleeping kits , hygiene kits , check-up , medicines and comprehensive case management .
The street dwellers are also being checked everyday for their body temperature .
Mayor Oca Malapitan has tasked the CSWD to ensure that the safety and well-being of them against the Covid-19 .
