Akeredolu advises residents to steer clear of Ondo explosion site  Governor Oluwarotimi Akeredolu of Ondo State has urged residents to steer clear of Saturday 's explosion site at Ilu Abo on the Akure-Owo Expressway .
The News Agency of Nigeria reports that a truck laden with dynamites being transported to Edo State from Lagos State had developed a fault and exploded around 1am .
The governor , who visited the scene of the explosion on Saturday , said the destruction caused by the explosion could only be imagined , advising residents to allow the bomb squad to do its job .
Akeredolu said : " I have had thorough briefing from head of our security agencies and the preliminary reports received was that it was an accidental discharge of explosives that was in transit through Ondo State to another state where it was needed .
" We will advice all citizens , all residents of Ondo State not to come near this place at all , they should vacate and move out of this premises .
" As we are told , the vehicle that was carrying this explosives is buried in the crater it created , @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ .
" Let everybody give the police , the bomb squad , space to do their work and we will know what to do .
" Akeredolu urged residents not to forget that the Coronavirus Disease was still raging , urging them to avoid crowded areas .
He said : " Right here where we are standing , we are not safe .
" Tell the people to move back and allow police to bring out the vehicle that is buried there .
" No casualty had been reported as at the time of filing this report .
