Lebanon closes parks ; Wilson County and Lebanon schools now closed through April 30  Lebanon Mayor Bernie Ash has issued a continuation of a state of emergency for the city in response to the coronavirus , according to an announcement Monday .
Ash first declared a state of emergency to go into effect on March 23 , in response to the novel coronavirus pandemic effective at midnight .

Lebanon also continues to prohibit social gatherings of 10 or more people .

on Tuesday and is a two-week statewide order closing non-essential businesses and telling Tennesseans to stay home in an effort to combat the spread of the coronavirus that lasts through April 14 .
Lee issued Executive Order 22 , implementing safer at home guidelines in every Tennessee county to further mitigate the spread of COVID-19 .
The order is not a mandated shelter in place , but instead urges Tennesseans who are in non-essential roles to remain at home , according to an announcement by the governor at a news briefing on Monday .
