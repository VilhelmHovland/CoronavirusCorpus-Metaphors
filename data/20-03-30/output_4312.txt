Political Analysis South Africa  Coronavirus lockdown : Transport restrictions eased for SASSA pay run  Coronavirus lockdown : Transport restrictions eased for SASSA pay run  Minister of Transport Fikile Mbalula has announced the easing of transport regulations for the coming week , following calls from the public for the government to cater for SASSA grant recipients .
On Sunday , 29 March 2020 , Minister Fikile Mbalula announced that as of Monday , 30 March 2020 , the existing transport regulations , particularly relating to public transport , will be eased to accommodate South African Social Security Agency ( SASSA ) grant recipients .
The initial regulations had allowed for public transport , inclusive of the Bus Rapid Transit ( BRT ) System and taxis , to be operational between 05:00 and 09:00 , and again from 16:0 until 20:00 daily .
However , between Monday , 30 March and Friday , 3 April 2020 , they will be permitted to operate from 05:00 until 20:00 .

In a tweet explaining his reasoning and forecasting for the week ahead , Minister Mbalula stated , " Unlike the privileged who were able to line up at big stores these last two weeks , theirs will be this week .
"  Grant recipients who will be travelling to and from their homes are required to carry their identification card or document and their SASSA card with them when they use public transport .
