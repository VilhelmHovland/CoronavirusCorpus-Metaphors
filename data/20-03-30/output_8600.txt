( photo credit : AMIR COHEN/REUTERS )  The Mossad purchased 10 million medical masks to protect against the coronavirus more than a week ago , The Jerusalem Post has learned .
Due to the sensitivity of the information , the Post could not report it until they arrived in Israel on Monday .
But it did note in a report on Friday that significant new acquisitions were on the way .
In addition to the 10 million medical masks , the Mossad brought a few dozen ventilators , tens of thousands of test kits and some 25,000 N95 surgical masks that are designed to protect the wearer from airborne particles and liquid .
