Total of 11 patients recover from coronavirus  Sri Lanka 's COVID-19 cases tally stood at 115 as of this evening while two patients made a full recovery during the course of the day .
This brings the total number of recovered cases to 11 .
In the meantime , the country reported its first COVID-19 death yesterday ; a 60-year-old male from Marawila area .
According to the situation report issued by the Epidemiology Unit of the Health Ministry this morning , 117 persons who are suspected to have contracted the virus are currently under observation at selected hospitals .
