The Chairman and Vice-Chairman of Famfa Oil Limited , Modupe and Folorunsho Alakija have donated N1bn to support the fight against coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) in Nigeria .
In a statement Sunday , the business philanthropists also announced the donation of N250m to the Lagos State Government and the Nigeria Centre for Disease Control ( NCDC ) respectively .
" As the world rallies to deal with the health , security , economic and social implications of the coronavirus , it 's clear that we will feel the effects much more deeply than many of the developed world .
Managing a crisis of this magnitude means that the strength of our response will determine our ability to weather the storm .
Individually and collectively , we are rising to this unprecedented challenge in a way that symbolizes our resilience , our character and strength , " the statement partly read .
Similarly , the medical workers on the frontline in Lagos and Abuja will each get N100m while another N100m will be for the supply of medical equipment to relevant organisations .
