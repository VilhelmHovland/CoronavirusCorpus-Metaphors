A sailor at the NSA Panama City has tested positive for COVID-19 .
( WJHG/WECP )  The sailor has started quarantine and is in isolation in their home under medical supervision .
The Florida Department of Health is working to notify those the sailor came in contact with that need to self-isolate .
Base officials tell us the health and safety of their sailors and staff is their number one priority and they are promoting social distancing by using teleworking when appropriate , and by using social distancing guidelines for those who have to come into work .
They are also continuing to promote good hygiene like washing work areas and washing hands in hopes to prevent the spread of COVID-19 .
