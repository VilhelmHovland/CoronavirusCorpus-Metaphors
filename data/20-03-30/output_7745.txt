Wyndham Hotels Plans to Cut 270 Jobs to Counter Coronavirus Effects  Wyndham Hotels &amp; Resorts ( WH ) said it needs to cut 270 jobs and plans to implement cost-cutting measures to cope with the negative effects of the global coronavirus pandemic .
The hotel operator said the cost-cutting steps are expected to lead to about $30 million in annual savings and will affect half of its 2,500 employees in the form of reduced salaries and reduced work hours .
The measures will be implemented in the second quarter of 2020 , Wyndham said in a SEC filing .
In addition , Chief Executive Geoffrey Ballotti will forgo 100% of his base compensation for 2020 , starting April 1 , and the board of directors will forgo the cash-based portion of their retainers .
