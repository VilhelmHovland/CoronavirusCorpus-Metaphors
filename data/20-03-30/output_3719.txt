IOC confirms new start date for Tokyo Olympic Games  The Tokyo Olympic Games , rescheduled for next year , has a new start date : July 23rd , 2021 .
The 2020 Olympic Games were postponed earlier this month amid the global outbreak of COVID-19 .
Since then , the International Olympic Committee ( IOC ) has set to find a replacement date during the 2021 summer .
This Monday , the IOC has confirmed that the Tokyo Olympics will now commence on July 23rd , 2021 , and will run until August 8th .
Meanwhile , the Paralympic Games will start on August 24th next year , concluding on September 6th .
The decision was made upon agreement between the IOC , the International Paralympic Committee ( IPC ) , the Tokyo 2020 Organising Committee , the Tokyo Metropolitan Government and the Government of Japan .
