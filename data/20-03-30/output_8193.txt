Moscow goes into lockdown , rest of Russia braces for same  Photo : ABC NEWS  Created : March 30 , 2020 02:15 PM  MOSCOW ( AP ) - The Russian capital has woken up to a lockdown obliging most people in the city of 13 million to stay home .
The government ordered other regions of the vast country to quickly prepare for the same as Moscow , to stem the spread of the new coronavirus .
Advertisement  Russia so far has been relatively spared by the outbreak , with 1,836 cases and nine deaths , but the past few days have seen an increase in the number of cases .
President Vladimir Putin on Monday warned his envoys in Russia 's far-flung regions that they will be personally responsible for the availability of beds , ventilators and other key equipment .
