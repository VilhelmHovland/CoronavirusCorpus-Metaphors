Dapur Jalanan keeps the homeless fed during shutdown  Share this article  DAPUR Jalanan Kuala Lumpur is a non-profit organisation running a soup kitchen for the homeless  Before the outbreak of the coronavirus , the group required many hands to help in the distribution of food and other aid for those in need , but it has since cut down the number of volunteers it recruits in keeping with the movement control order imposed on March 18 .
Last weekend in Jalan Panggong saw hundreds of homeless receiving food , hand sanitiser and other essentials from the volunteers .
