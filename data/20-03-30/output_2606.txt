Teenager allegedly robs woman of valuables  An 18-year-old bricklayer , Damilare Dosu , who allegedly robbed a woman of her valuables at a cutlass-point , was on Friday brought before an Ikeja Magistrates ' Court .
Dosu , whose address was not provided , is facing a twocount charge of conspiracy and robbery .
The accused , Police Prosecutor Ishola Samuel said , committed the offences with others still at large on Aug .
27 at 6:03 a .
at No 6 Isah Olusesi St .
, Alagbado , a Lagos suburb .
Samuel said the accused , armed with a cutlass , robbed the complainant , Miss Mistura Ogundairo of her Techno phones valued at N38,000 .
" The complainant was at the gate of her house waiting for a motorcycle to her place of work when she was attacked .
" The accused used the cutlass to attack her before dispossessing her of a handbag containing the phones , " he told the court .
The offences contravened Sections 297 and 411 of the Criminal Law of Lagos State , 2015 .
