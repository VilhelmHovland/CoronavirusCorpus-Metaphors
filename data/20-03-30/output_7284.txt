Breadcrumb  FDA authorizes emergency use of rapid response COVID-19 test  Mar 30 , 2020 - 04:53 PM  The Food and Drug Administration Friday authorized emergency use of the ID NOW Instrument in patient care settings to quickly test for COVID-19 through direct nasal or throat swab testing .
According to the manufacturer , Abbott , the test may detect positive results in five minutes and negative results in 13 minutes .
