The Board of Directors of Special Olympics South Dakota have informed the Vermillion Organizing Committee that the 2020 State Summer Games have been canceled due to the COVID-19 outbreak .
Officials said the nature of this world-wide crisis is ever-changing , and with the safety of the athletes and volunteers being at the forefront of priorities , it has been decided the games will be canceled for the year .
With so much uncertainty and many moving parts , Special Olympics International has determined that rescheduling the event this year is not possible .
" Our State Summer Games is always a really exceptional event for everyone , and we are devastated that we ca n't have it this year , " President and CEO of Special Olympics South Dakota Darryl Nordquist said .
" While we are sad to have to cancel , we are looking forward to holding the 2021 State Summer Games in Vermillion next spring .
