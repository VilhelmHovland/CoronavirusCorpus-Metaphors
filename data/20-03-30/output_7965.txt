- Panhandle Public Health District confirmed that a male in his 50s residing in Kimball County has tested positive for COVID-19 .
This case is travel-related to Colorado , and the person is currently hospitalized .
Contact investigations are underway to identify people who may have come into close contact with the individual for the past 14 days to help prevent further spread .
All identified close contacts will self-quarantine and be actively monitored twice daily by public health officials for fever and respiratory symptoms .
