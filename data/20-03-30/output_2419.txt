AHEIA makes Alberta Fishing Education Program available free of charge  The Alberta Hunter Education Instructors ' Association is making its Alberta Fishing Education program available online and free to the public .
The release of the program is for those who are self-isolating because of COVID-19 .
It normally costs $40 .
Robert Gruszecki with the Alberta Hunter Education Instructors ' Association says the program is great for those interested in learning about the province 's wildlife .
" We feel now is the perfect time to release the Alberta Fishing Education Program for free , so that all Albertans can take advantage of this extra time they may have found themselves with while they self-isolate or quarantine at home away from school and work .
"  The program is the only program of its kind offered in North America .
Its purpose is to provide confidence through understanding to better enable more people to responsibly spend time outdoors and enjoy and preserve the province 's wildlife .
