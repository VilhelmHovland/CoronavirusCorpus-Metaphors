Actions  1,221 positive coronavirus cases in Wisconsin , 14 deaths reported  MADISON ( NBC 26 ) -- 1,221 positive coronavirus cases have now been reported in Wisconsin , with 14 deaths , according to an update from the state Monday afternoon .
The Governor , citing health officials , said more cases are expected as more tests are conducted .
The Governor also renewed a call for donations for PPEs ( Personal Protective Equipment ) .
Governor Tony Evers was joined by Department of Health Services Secretary-designee Andrea Palm and Chief Medical Officer Dr .
Ryan Westergaard to answer questions about the state 's response to COVID-19 in Wisconsin , the testing process , and additional steps people can take to avoid illness .
