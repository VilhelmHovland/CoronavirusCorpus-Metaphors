Getting started  The command center for online classes will be Launchpad , accessible by clicking the icon on the school district 's homepage , brevardschools .
You will need your student 's log-in email and school password to access .
The Launchpad page features links to the applications your student will use to complete assignments , including the BPS FOCUS app , which should always be your first stop after logging in .
Through FOCUS , you can keep track of your student 's weekly assignments and their due dates , which can be viewed in the box marked " Alerts .
" FOCUS can also be used to upload completed assignments , when necessary .
Attendance will automatically be logged by signing into FOCUS , so students should access the app daily .
Students are not required to log in at a certain time of day and may work through materials and complete assignments when it is convenient for @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ FOCUS and instead work directly with their child 's teacher .
Lessons and assignments  Lessons will be delivered in a variety of formats , including recorded video , animations , text and others .
Teachers have been given broad leeway on how to deliver instruction and may supplement the school district 's curriculum with their own materials , so contact your child 's teacher if you have questions about how to access lessons .
Instructions for completing most assignments can be found in the weekly lesson plans , accessible through FOCUS by clicking the " Instructional Continuity Plan " link under " Messages , " scrolling down the page and clicking on your student 's grade level .
Lessons have been stripped down to avoid overwhelming families and assignments will be due on a weekly basis with no set hours for completion , allowing you and your student to work around your own schedules .
Teachers will continue to grade assignments and students will be issued report cards as normal .

Your teacher will check in weekly to find out how families are adjusting to the new online format and make sure students are staying on track .
They 'll also be available during specific hours , Monday through Friday , to answer questions and provide support .
Contact your teacher to find out when they 're available and how best to get in touch .
For other questions , you can reach your child 's school by phone between 9 a .

Other resources , including answers to frequently asked questions and support on subjects from remote instruction to student services and ESE support , can found in the Parent Help Guide .
Access the guide by clicking on the " Parent Help Guide " link at the top of the " Instructional Continuity Plan " page , accessible through FOCUS .
