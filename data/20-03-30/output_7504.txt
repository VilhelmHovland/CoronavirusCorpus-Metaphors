Soon , medical personnel in Pennsylvania and perhaps New Jersey and New York will be wearing basic protective masks and gowns made from the polyester pinstripe cloth that originally was intended for Nike-branded Major League Baseball uniforms .
Instead of using the material to make Yankees and Phillies attire for major leaguers and for commercial retail sales , online sports apparel giant Fanatics said Thursday it would restart a domestic plant to instead create protective gear for emergency personnel faced with shortages of such supplies while battling the coronavirus pandemic .
