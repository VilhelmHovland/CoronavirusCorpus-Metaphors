Macy 's will furlough most of its 125,000 workers amid coronavirus pandemic  By Anne D'Innocenzio  Associated Press  Mar 30 , 2020 3:17 PM  New York  Macy 's says it will temporarily stop paying tens of thousands of employees who were thrown out of work when the chain closed its stores in response to collapsing sales during the coronavirus pandemic .
The majority of its 125,000 employees , including stock people and sales clerks , will still collect health benefits , but the company said that it is transitioning to an " absolute minimum workforce " needed to maintain basic operations .
Macy 's has lost the bulk of its sales due to the temporarily closing of all 500 of its stores that started March 18 .
