Michelle Hueffmeier with the Clarksville-Montgomery County Economic Development Council said Monday it 's too early to pin a number or percentage to the decline Clarksville 's hospitality industry is experiencing along with the coronavirus outbreak .
More specifics will be known about that in a matter of weeks , she said , since hotel-motel tax revenue reports generally run about two months behind real-time .
The problem is hardly exclusive to Clarksville .
The nearby tourism hotbed of Nashville already reports that it 's downtown hotel occupancy has dropped below 10% for the first time on record .
In Memphis , hotels have seen bookings drop by about 75% in less than one week .
In Knoxville , five major event cancellations have emptied hotels and cost the city about $24 million in revenue .
