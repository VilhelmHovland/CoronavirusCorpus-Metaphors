Eskom moves to reassure lenders over Covid-19 lockdown  Eskom moved to reassure lenders and markets on Monday that it will continue to meet its financial commitments and obligations during and after South Africa 's Covid-19 lockdown .
In a statement issued through the JSE , the indebted state-owned electricity utility said it " recognises the gravity of the recent outbreak of Covid-19 , and the difficulties the country will have to endure in our endeavours to arrest the spread of the pandemic " .
" We would like to assure our lenders that as an essential and critical services company , Eskom 's operations will continue unimpeded by the national lockdown .
Accordingly , please be assured that financial commitments and obligations during and after the lockdown will be honoured as they fall due .
In this tumultuous period , our partnerships matter more than ever , and we are grateful for your continued support , " it said .
