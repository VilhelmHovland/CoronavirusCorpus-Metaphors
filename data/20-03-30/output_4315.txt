President Cyril Ramaphosa has thanked Wuhan , China , for its safe-keeping of the 114 repatriates that returned home safely , stating that South Africa , as a nation , can learn from Wuhan 's safety measures .
On Monday , 30 March 2020 , President Cyril Ramaphosa released a statement confirming that the 114 South Africans that were repatriated from Wuhan , China , are now safe to go home .
The repatriates endured a two-week quarantine upon their return to South Africa .
The two weeks they spent in quarantine in South Africa made a total of 75 days in quarantine at Polokwane 's Ranch Hotel , after having spent 51 days in containment in Wuhan .
In his statement , the president stated that there are lessons that South Africa can learn from how the city handled the outbreak , further stating that the national lockdown is necessary .
His statement stressed that , " It was due to the drastic actions that the Chinese government took to contain the disease in the city of Wuhan , that all of our people were able to return uninfected and healthy .
