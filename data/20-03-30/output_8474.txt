Havelock man accused of COVID-19 hoax  CRAVEN COUNTY , N .
( WITN ) - A man from Havelock is accused of posting on Facebook that he was going to infect people with coronavirus .
According to the Craven County Sheriff 's Office , 44-year-old Christopher Floyd is charged with disorderly conduct .
Deputies say citizens reported that his post said he had COVID-19 and would infect others .
Investigators determined that he did not have coronavirus and posted it online as a hoax .
