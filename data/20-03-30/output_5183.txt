Chennai:Tamil Nadu on Friday reported six COVID-19 positive cases , taking the total number of cases in the state to 35 , even as Chief Minister 's office consented to the recruitment of additional workorce to handle the crisis , Health Minister C Vijayabaskar said .
The latest positive cases in the state include two family members of a patient who was admitted to Government Rajaji Hospital in Madurai and two contacts of Thailand nationals admitted to IRT Perundurai Medical College Hospital in Erode district .
Others are a contact of a patient in Chennai and a 25-year-old woman from Chennai , who is admitted to Government Hospital in Ariyalur .
One patient admitted to Rajiv Gandhi Government General Hospital was discharged last week while a 54-year-old man admitted to Government Rajaji Hospital in Madurai died in the early hours of Wednesday .
Vijayabaskar said that Chief Minister Edappadi K Palaniswami 's office has consented to the recruitment of additional workorce through Medical Services Recruitment Board ( MRB ) abiding the rules and regulations .
