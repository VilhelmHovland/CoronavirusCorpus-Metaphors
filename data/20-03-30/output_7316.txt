California-based Virgin Orbit has teamed up with researchers to make a ventilator device to help fill a critical shortage in the U .
as it battles the growing coronavirus outbreak .
According to a report in CNBC , the company that is owned by Richard Branson is working to get approval from the Food and Drug Administration ( FDA ) that it hopes will be just a few days .
The company hopes to get the devices into hospitals as soon as possible and make hundreds of thousands a week near term .
The device specifically compresses medical bag valve masks , which help patients infected by coronavirus breathe .
