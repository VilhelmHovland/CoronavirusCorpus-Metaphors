A Music Video Perfect for Social Distancing  Over 10 years ago , art director Masashi Kawamura teamed up with Japanese band Sour to produce a fantastic music video for their song " Hibi no Neiro " ( " Everyday Tone " ) .
Needless to say , this was before our current reality , in which millions are staying home due to the coronavirus outbreak .
In the video , a cast , selected from the band 's actual fan base , choreograph a perfectly-timed dance that was filmed purely via webcam from locations all over the world .
It 's the perfect music video for the age of social distancing .
