Uttar Pradesh  Uttarakhand  West Bengal  NYOOOZ  My City My News  Cotton University teachers donate one day salary to Relief Fund of Assam Government  Sunday 29th March , 2020  Staff ReporterGUWAHATI : The teaching fraternity of Cotton University ( CU ) will donate one day salary to the Relief Fund of State Government , to boost the fight against COVID-19 .
The teaching fraternity expressed their satisfaction towards the State government for their actions to tackle the outbreak of COVID-19 .
The teachers have already expressed their interest in a letter to the Health and Finance Minister Himanta Biswa Sarma .
President Dr .
Suresh Kumar Nath and Secretary Dr .
Ruma Talukdar of Cotton University Teachers ' Association expressed their gratitude for the generosity showed by the teaching fraternity of the varsity .
