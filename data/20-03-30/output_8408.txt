" Clarence House has confirmed today that , having consulted with his doctor , the Prince of Wales is now out of self-isolation , " a royal spokesman said in a statement .
Prince Charles , the heir to the British throne , is out of isolation and in good health after contracting coronavirus .
Charles , 71 , tested positive for Covid-19 last week and had been self-isolating at his residence in Scotland .
