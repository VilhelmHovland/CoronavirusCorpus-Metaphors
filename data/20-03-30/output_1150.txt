Business secretary Alok Sharma has announced a package of new measures aimed at ensuring companies are " ready to bounce back " when the coronavirus crisis passes .
Changes to insolvency rules , a temporary suspension of wrongful trading provisions for directors to remove the threat of personal liability , as well as plans to allow companies to hold their annual general meetings online are among a range of proposals to be announced .
The development comes the number of people in the UK who have died after contracting coronavirus passed 1,000 .
The announcement came as Mr Sharma insisted there are " no gaps in government " despite Boris Johnson and three key figures entering self-isolation over coronavirus .
