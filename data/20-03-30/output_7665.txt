The Securities and Exchange Commission has announced two meetings of Committees that provide recommendations to the Commission .
The Small Business Capital Formation Advisory Committee on Small and Emerging Companies Meeting and the Investor Advisory Committee will both be holding meetings on Thursday , April 2 , 2020 .
Both meetings will take place as a video conference .
The two Committees are expected to discuss the Impact of the COVID-19 outbreak on both investors and smaller firms .
The Committees may provide recommendations to the Commission in light of the crisis .
The Small Business Capital Formation Advisory Committee will take place at 12 Noon ET and the Investor Advisory Committee will take place at 4 PM .
