Actions  Appleton reports second case of coronavirus  APPLETON ( NBC 26 ) -- Appleton has reported its second case of coronavirus .
According to a statement from the city , the Appleton Health Department received notice Monday of a second person in Appleton who tested positive for COVID-19 .
The person , who lives in Outagamie County , contracted the virus through travel .
The city 's statement said the person remains home in isolation .
The resident has been compliant with all requests from Appleton 's health department and is following all recommendations to prevent contact with others .
The health department is working to contact any people who had close contact with the ill individual .
Close contacts will be educated about self-monitoring their health status by health department nurses .
They will be required to do daily symptom and temperature checks ; as well as quarantine themselves for 14 day as a precaution .
