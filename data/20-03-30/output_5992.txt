The Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation ( BMC ) on Monday ordered that bodies of all the coronavirus victims should be cremated irrespective of religion .
No burial will be allowed , Praveen Pardeshi , the BMC commissioner said today .
Pardeshi also clarified that anyone insisting on burying a body would only be permitted if it is taken out of Mumbai city 's jurisdiction .
At least 47 new patients have tested positive for coronavirus in the Mumbai Metropolitan Region ( MMR ) .
In Maharashtra , a total of 39 people have been discharged from hospitals so far after recovering from the novel coronavirus infection .
