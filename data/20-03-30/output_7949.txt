JMCGH has four COVID-19 hospitalizations  Jackson-Madison County General Hospital is treating four patients for the novel coronavirus .
The patients are from Chester , Benton and Madison Counties .
West Tennessee Healthcare Communications Director Amy Garner said the hospital has enough equipment to treat patients , but is concerned about a spike in patients that could overwhelm rural West Tennessee 's largest hospital .
" The equipment is adequate for now , " she said .
" The concern is a sudden spike in critically ill patients and that 's why we need to spread it out over time .
