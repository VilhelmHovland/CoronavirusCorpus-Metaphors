San Simon limits attendance of funeral wakes  AS PART of the measures implemented to contain the spread of coronavirus , the local government in San Simon town asked residents to limit funeral wakes to immediate family only .
This is to prevent gathering of people which will be in violation of the social distancing strongly recommended by the Department of Health amid the threat of coronavirus disease ( Covid-19 ) , said Mayor Abundio " Jun " Punsalan , Jr .
" We ask relatives and friends of the deceased to suspend for the meantime their visit to the wake and just pray for their soul at their respective own houses instead , " he said .
Immediate family members are also advised to maintain social distancing during the wake and if possible , avoid walking during the funeral going to the cemetery .
" If possible , we ask families to let the body be blessed at their homes and then bring them directly to the cemetery , " he said .
Punsalan said he understands the feeling of wanting to be with the departed loved one for the final moment @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ prioritized at the moment .
He also clarified that funeral wakes are only allowed for non-Covid-19 related death , as those who died of the disease are instructed to cremate the bodies immediately .
" I understand the sadness and pain of losing your loved ones but we have to abide by the preventive measures we are implementing to safeguard you , your families and friends and community from the deadly disease , " Punsalan said .
