Taking to Twitter , TMC MP raised several questions over Centre 's handling of the issue and asked where is the pan-India plan to help the migrant workers stuck with no jobs or food ?  Thousand of migrant workers are leaving for their hometowns from their workplaces in the wake of 21-day lockdown enforced by the Centre to stop the spread of viral infection .
A total of 1024 people have been infected with COVID-19 in the country so far , while 27 have died due to the disease , as per the latest data given by the Ministry of Health &amp; Family Welfare .

However , soon after the announcement thousands of migrant workers across the country started leaving for their native places in the absence of work due to the lockdown .
Several steps and measures have been announced by both the States and the Centre to stop the mass exodus of migrants in the wake of the migrant crisis .

" Action should be taken against those who are asking labourers or students to vacate the premises " , the Ministry of Information and Broadcasting said in a statement .
