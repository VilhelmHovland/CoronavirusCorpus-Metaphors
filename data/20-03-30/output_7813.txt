Disney Channel Stars Record Positive Messages Amid COVID-19  Stars from various Disney Channel shows have participated in a series of new interstitial messages entitled We 're All in This Together .
Designed to offer reassurance and a sense of community during the current global health crisis , the series includes over 35 Disney Channel stars who each filmed their message from their homes and around their neighborhoods .
They share how they stay positive , active and navigate through this uncertain time with the help of parents and family members .
