Sanchez : Let 's thank health care workers  BRONX , N .
-- The New York Yankees do a great job of honoring Veterans and members of our military during the seventh-inning stretch .
During the tradition , the team has played " God Bless America , " at Yankee Stadium , following the 9/11 terrorist attacks .
Yankees catcher Gary Sanchez tweeted out that when the COVID-19 pandemic ends and we get back to baseball , those health care workers on the front lines should be thanked for their heroism .
When and however possible , we should all take the time to thank the health workers who are helping those infected with COVID-19 and trying to control and cure the virus so that we can all go back to our normal lives .
They are true heroes that deserve all our gratitude .
