Stars of ' Contagion ' reunite for public service announcements  NEW YORK -- The stars of the 2011 virus thriller " Contagion " -- a prescient film these days -- have reunited for a series of public service announcements to warn about COVID-19 .
Matt Damon , Laurence Fishburne , Kate Winslet and Jennifer Ehle have teamed up with scientists from Columbia University 's Mailman School of Public Health to offer four individual homemade videos .
