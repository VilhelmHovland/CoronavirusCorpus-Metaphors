Article Tools  BLOOMING GROVE -- A staff member at the Pike County Correctional Facility tested positive for COVID-19 six days ago and has been quarantined at home since March 18 , according to a statement from the county commissioners .
That staff member was not identified .
Though the employee tested positive March 24 , the county issued a statement Sunday night after " recent requests for information regarding the status of individuals at the Pike County Correctional Facility .
"  There are no positive COVID-19 cases among jail inmates or detainees , the commissioners said .
The inmates who had direct contact with the infected employee are quarantined .
Other staff who had contact with the person are also home under self-quarantine .
" Elevated cleaning and sanitizing protocols which had been in place will continue , " according to the statement .
" Inmates and staff are reminded to frequently wash their hands , and to cover their coughs and sneezes .
