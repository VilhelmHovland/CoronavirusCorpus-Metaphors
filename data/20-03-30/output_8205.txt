WEST HAVEN -- The city has closed the popular 1 .
mile shoreline boardwalk until further notice in response to Gov .
Ned Lamont 's executive order restricting social and recreational gatherings to no more than five people , according to city officials .
Health Director Maureen B .
Lillis said the city closed the walkway , which goes from Bradley Point to Sandy Point to discourage the spread of the novel coronavirus , the release states .
The boardwalk often draws thousands from West Haven and other areas , and walking on it was viewed by many as one of the few activities left during these times of social distancing .
