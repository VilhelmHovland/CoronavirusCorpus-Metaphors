General public should coordinate with the government to counter Covid-19 pandemic :  The Punjab government is vigilant enough to counter any worse situation but the government alone can not overcome this crisis .
It is responsibility of the general public to coordinate with the competent authorities to counter Covid-19 pandemic .
Chief Minister Sardar Usman Buzdar is personally monitoring the situation all over the province .
The Chief Minister is in contact with the medical teams , media , security and law enforcement agencies to contain the Coronavirus pandemic .
Unfortunately , the previous governments did not take effective measures to improve the condition of healthcare sector .
The Punjab hospitals are in short of ventilators and medical equipment .
It is criminal negligence of the previous governments to ignore health sector .
