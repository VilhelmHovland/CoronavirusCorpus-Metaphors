Chanel produces face masks to help combat coronavirus  Prototypes are being worked on and will be signed off by French authorities  Mar 30 , 2020  Claudio LaveniaGetty Images  Chanel will launch face masks as the brand joins the fight against coronavirus .
The luxury fashion house is currently working on prototypes which will be signed off by the French authorities .
French health minister Olivier Veran said France is currently using 40 million face masks each week and has ordered more than a billion more , mostly from China .
" Today we are mobilising our workforce and our partners .
to produce protective masks and blouses , " Chanel said in a statement .
The fashion house also said it will continue to employ its 4,500 staff despite challenges that the pandemic has placed on businesses .
