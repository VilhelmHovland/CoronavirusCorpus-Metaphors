Peter Dejong/Associated Press  THE HAGUE , Netherlands -- A Dutch museum that is currently closed to prevent the spread of the coronavirus said Monday a painting by Vincent van Gogh on loan for an exhibition was stolen in a raid overnight .
The Singer Laren museum east of Amsterdam says " Spring Garden " by the Dutch master was taken in the early hours of Monday .
Museum director Evert van Os said the institution that houses the collection of American couple William and Anna Singer is " angry , shocked , sad " at the theft of the painting .
The value of the work , which was on loan from the Groninger Museum in the northern Dutch city of Groningen , was not immediately known .
Police are investigating the theft .
