New Delhi : The Indian Council of Medical Research ( ICMR ) , the nation 's apex biomedical research body , on Wednesday , said that 581 people have tested positive for the SARS-CoV-2 pathogen that causes the novel coronavirus .
" A total of 25,144 samples from 24,254 individuals have been tested for SARS-CoV2 as on March 25 , 8:00 pm , " a joint statement by ICMR and Union Health Ministry said .
" A total of 581 individuals have been confirmed positive among suspected cases and contacts of known positive cases , " it added .
