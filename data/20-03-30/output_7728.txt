Macy 's announced Monday that it will furlough the " majority " of its employees companywide to balance the checkbooks after the coronavirus pandemic has closed down stores and sent sales plummeting .
The department store did not specify how many workers would be let go , but the company employs about 125,000 people , according to its website .
Advertisement  " We will be moving to the absolute minimum workforce needed to maintain basic operations , " Macy 's said in a statement .
All Macy 's stores shut down on March 18 and " will remain closed until we have clear line of sight on when it is safe to reopen , " the company said .
All Macy 's stores have been closed since March 18 .
( Susan Walsh/AP )  The retailer is still selling online , but sales have plummeted during the pandemic .
" We 've already taken measures to maintain financial flexibility , including suspending the dividend , drawing down our line of credit , freezing both hiring and spending , stopping capital spend , reducing receipts , cancelling some orders and extending payment @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ , " Macy 's said in a statement .
Furloughed employees will still keep their health insurance through at least May , the company said .
