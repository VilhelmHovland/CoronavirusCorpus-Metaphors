Batanes PLGU ensures enough supply of rice amid COVID-19 scare  TUGUEGARAO CITY , Cagayan , March 29 ( PIA ) - - - The Provincial Government of Batanes has assured Ivatans that the province has enough rice for the month-long enhanced community quarantine that was implemented due to the threat of the coronavirus disease 2019 ( COVID -- 19 ) .
Acting Governor Ignacio Villa said his constituents should not panic as he undestands every Ivatan 's plight especially their basic needs .
Villa said there is enough supply of rice in the market as the provincial government is continuously coordinating with local rice suppliers .
As of writing , 2,000 sacks of National Food Authority ( NFA ) rice is on the way , as per information from NFA - Batanes .
Jehan Gecha , NFA Batanes oic said additional 2,000 sacks of rice have been requested for release and shipment on top of the 2,000 sacks now in transit , pending accomplishment of necessary documents .
