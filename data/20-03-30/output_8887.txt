Kurdistan announces 8 new corona infections  Shafaq News / The Ministry of Health of Kurdistan Regional Government announced on Monday morning , the diagnosis of new infections with the emerging corona virus .
A statement of the Ministry stated to Shafaq News , " Eight cases of Corona were diagnosed in Erbil city .
The statement explained that the infections were distributed to a man , a woman and three children , all of whom were relatives of a case that was diagnosed yesterday in Khulaifan sub-district of a man returning from Britain .
He added , that the other three infections of people from Dohuk who returned from France recently were quarantined in Erbil .
This brings the number of Corona infections in the region to 150 , including two deaths and 55 recovering cases .
