According to Nauman Ijaz , he thought only the people of Pakistan were ignorant when it came to basic living but he has now realized otherwise .
" Corona has unveiled one thing .
Earlier I used to think ignorance was only present in Pakistan .
But with Corona now I understood that even those who speak English are pretty ignorant as well , " says the Mera Saaein actor .
