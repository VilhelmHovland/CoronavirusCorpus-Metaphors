KINGSTON , Jamaica -- Prime Minister Andrew Holness has announced that effective , Wednesday , April 1 there will be an all island curfew starting at 8:00 each night and ending at 6:00 am the following day .

Updating the nation on Government 's decisions to tackle the virus on Friday , Holness said he had assigned Cabinet ministers Daryl Vaz and Desmond McKenzie the responsibility of directly overseeing the trucking of water .
" What we will have to do as a backup measure is to increase our spending , our organisation , and logistics around trucking .
So , what I have decided to do is to assign minister Vaz and minister McKenzie the responsibility to directly oversee the trucking of water .
Now , the responsibilities will be divided into two , " he said .
