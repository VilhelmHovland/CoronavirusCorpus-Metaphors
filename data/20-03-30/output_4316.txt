Political Analysis South Africa  Death of KZN resident brings South Africa 's COVID-19 death toll to two  Death of KZN resident brings South Africa 's COVID-19 death toll to two  Published on  30 March , 2020  The National Institute for Communicable Diseases ( NICD ) has confirmed that a KwaZulu-Natal ( KZN ) resident has passed away due to the coronavirus , bringing the country 's death toll to two .
In a Twitter post on Sunday , 29 March 2020 , the NICD confirmed that another COVID-19 victim succumbed to the virus earlier that day .
The post stated , " We have unfortunately recorded a new COVID-19-related death in KwaZulu-Natal , bringing the number of COVID-19-related deaths to two in the country .
We send our deepest condolences to the family and friends of the deceased .
"  The death of the KZN resident follows the announcement of the first recorded death on Friday , 27 March 2020 .
The deceased was 48-year-old female who tested positive on Monday , 23 March 2020 .
