Valley View has extended its closure through April 16 , officials for the casino announced on Monday , March 30 .
The casino previously said it was hoping to reopen April 3 .
The Agua Caliente Band of Cahuilla Indians announced that their casino properties would now remain closed through April 12 .
The casino properties originally planned to stay closed through the end of March .
