Kyle Free murder trial postponed  GRAND JUNCTION , Colo .
( KKCO/KJCT ) -- The trial for a man accused of murdering Kyle Free has been pushed back because of the COVID-19 pandemic .
Lawyers say the trial for John Eddy has been pushed back until August 10th .
It was supposed to start March 30th .
Eddy 's defense attorneys filed a motion to continue the trial later this summer because of coronavirus concerns .
Eddy is the man accused of shooting Kyle Free back in April of 2018 .
David Castro , who prosecutors alleged was with Eddy at the time , was found guilty of second degree murder in Free 's death late last year .
Free 's girlfriend , Rebecca Walker pleaded guilty to second degree murder in 2019 .
She is currently serving a 51 year prison sentence .
