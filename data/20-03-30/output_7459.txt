CCISD : Children do n't need to be present for meal pickup  Corpus Christi ISD will no longer require children to be present for its free curbside meal service .
The meals are offered to any child 18 and younger while schools are closed to protect against the coronavirus .
The to-go meals , consisting of one breakfast and one lunch per child , are available from 10:30 a .
to 1 p .
