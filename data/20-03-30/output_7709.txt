10 residents of 7 Hidalgo Co .
cities test positive for coronavirus  EDINBURG -- 10 more people in Hidalgo County have tested positive for coronavirus , bringing the total number of people diagnosed with the disease to 38 , Hidalgo County Judge Richard F .
Cortez said Monday .
The new cases include a 45-year-old male and a 14-year-old male from Mission , a 37-year-old male from San Juan , a 27-year-old male and a 64-year-old female from Pharr , a 32-year-old male and a 77-year-old female from McAllen , a 60-year-old female from Weslaco , a 30-year-old female from Mercedes and a 19-year-old female from Donna .
Investigators are still trying to determine whether the cases are travel-related as well as where the infected patients have been recently in the community .
