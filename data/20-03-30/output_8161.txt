Bangladesh garment makers say $3 billion in orders lost due to coronavirus  Bangladesh garment manufacturers says fashion retailers have cancelled or put on hold more than $3 billion in orders due to the coronavirus outbreak , though a handful have agreed to pay anyway .
Fashion Bangladesh garment  The data from the Bangladesh Garment Manufacturers and Exporters Association released Monday reflected both orders already made or in the works and planned orders from the country , which is the world 's second largest exporter of clothing after China .
The cancelled orders , according to reports to the BGMEA from manufacturers , included tens of millions in purchases from many big buyers , including European buyers C&amp;A and Inditex , Primark of Ireland , Britain 's Marks &amp; Spencer and Tesco and U .
retailers like Walmart and Target .
