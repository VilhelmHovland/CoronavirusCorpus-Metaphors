Coronavirus ' Clusters Of Concern ' Form As MD Stays Home : Patch PM  Hours before a statewide stay-at-home order took effect at 8 p .
Monday in Maryland , state officials explained why it was necessary .
" I want to tell you right now that there are some very sick people here in Maryland , " Deputy Health Secretary Fran Phillips said at a news conference at the State House .
" Today we had 67 new hospitalizations in the last 24 hours due to the new coronavirus , " Phillips said .
" We have a deadly outbreak in Carroll County , and we know that there are clusters of concern under investigation around the state .
"  Nursing homes , detention centers and others were among areas Gov .
Larry Hogan said were of concern .
" There are literally thousands of facilities we 're concerned about in the state of Maryland , " Hogan said .
