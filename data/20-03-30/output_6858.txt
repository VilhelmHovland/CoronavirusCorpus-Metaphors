This decision was taken based on three main considerations and in line with the principles established by the IOC Executive Board ( EB ) on 17 March 2020 and confirmed at its meeting today .
These were supported by all the International Summer Olympic Sports Federations ( IFs ) and all the National Olympic Committees ( NOCs ) :  1 .
To protect the health of the athletes and everyone involved , and to support the containment of the COVID-19 virus .
To safeguard the interests of the athletes and of Olympic sport .
The global international sports calendar .
