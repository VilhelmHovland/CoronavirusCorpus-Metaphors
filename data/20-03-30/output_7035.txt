EPA encourages Americans to only flush toilet paper  Monday  Mar 30 , 2020 at 6:22 PM  WASHINGTON -- Today , the U .
Environmental Protection Agency is encouraging all Americans to only flush toilet paper , not disinfecting wipes or other non-flushable items that should be disposed of in the trash , according to a communication forwarded to town residents by Wareham officials .
Flushing only toilet paper helps ensure that the toilets , plumbing , sewer systems and septic systems will continue working properly to safely manage our nation 's wastewater .
While EPA encourages disinfecting your environment to prevent the spread of COVID-19 , never flush disinfecting wipes or other non-flushable items .
These easy steps will keep surfaces disinfected and wastewater management systems working for all Americans .
Preventable toilet and sewer backups can pose a threat to human health and present an extra challenge to our water utilities and their workforce .
Flushing anything other than toilet paper , including disinfecting wipes , can damage internal plumbing , local sewer systems and septic systems .
