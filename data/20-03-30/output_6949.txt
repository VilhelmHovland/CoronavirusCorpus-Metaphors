Mar 30 , 2020 10:28 am ET  Joint meeting of the Select Board , School Committee , Board of Health , and Parks &amp; Recreation Commission  Due to the ongoing COVID-19 Pandemic and State of Emergency , on March 12 , 2020 , Governor Baker issued an Executive Order Temporarily Suspending Certain Provisions of the Open Meeting Law , G .
30A sec .
Pursuant to the Order , public bodies are temporarily relieved from the Open Meeting Law 's requirement that meetings be held in public places , open and physically accessible to the public , so long as measures are taken to ensure public access to the bodies ' deliberations " through adequate , alternative means .
