Needles City Council declares local emergency over virus pandemic  NEEDLES -- The Needles City Council has declared a local emergency due to COVID-19 .
According to background information provided by city staff , the City and Needles Public Utility Authority have incurred additional costs to respond which can be reimbursed .
The City/NPUA also will experience losses in sales and transient occupancy taxes , lost revenue from event closure such as the rodeo and the boat show and golf course and utility revenues as a result of business closures .
City Manager Rick Daniels stated during the meeting that there are about 2,200 residential accounts and about 417 commercial accounts in the utility department .
" Commercial accounts , although much smaller in number , represents 56% of sales , " said Daniels .

The resolution states that a proclamation of local emergency will assist in coordinated public health response to reduce transmission and illness severity , assist health care providers , coordinate and mitigate public services that may be distributed from this emergency and mitigate any other effects of this emergency on the citizens of the city .
The background information states that there are two pieces of legislation , with a third under consideration , that will reimburse businesses for the economic costs of responding to the virus and providing compensation for individuals who were furloughed or lost their jobs as a result of the virus .
The state , county and local declarations will best qualify those businesses and individuals for compensation .
The local emergency shall remain in effect until such time that the council determines that the emergency conditions have bee abated .
The city council approved Resolution 2020-14 declaring the existence of a local emergency due to COVID-19 .
