LIST : Who can avail of the gov't social amelioration program ?  President Rodrigo Duterte is allocating P200 billion for low-income families who have been hard hit by the coronavirus pandemic .
The 18 million families from the informal sectors are assured of benefitting from the emergency subsidies , according to the Bayanihan to Heal as One Act , which grants Duterte additional powers to implement measures to prevent the spread of the coronavirus .
Each household can receive up to P8,000 in financial assistance for two months .
In a virtual press briefing Tuesday , Cabinet Secretary Karlo Nograles said the aid would be immediately distributed by the Department of Social Welfare and Development to the residents impacted by the outbreak .
