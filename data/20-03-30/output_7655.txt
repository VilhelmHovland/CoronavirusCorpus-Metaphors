Republic Services has implemented new rules for garbage pickup in Crest Hill starting Monday due to the coronavirus pandemic , the city announced .
Pickup of yard waste and bulk items has been suspended .
Republic Services will only accept garbage and recycling , and drivers will not leave their trucks to pick up waste outside of the carts .
Customers who do not have Republic 's carted service must place waste in bags that can not exceed 50 pounds .
