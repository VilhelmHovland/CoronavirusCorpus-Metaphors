Total global ad spend declined slightly by mid-March : PubMatic report  The report by the digital technology company summarises the impact of COVID-19 on global ad spend .
The impact varied greatly based on the advertiser category .
As the Coronavirus pandemic disrupts every aspect of our daily lives , the PubMatic team has been monitoring its impact on global digital ad spend .
The total global ad spend declined slightly by mid-March , as many of the world 's largest economies reacted to the COVID-19 threat .
According to the PubMatic report , the impact varied greatly based on advertiser category .
Travel , Sports and Science categories experienced the steepest ad spend declines , while News , Hobbies and Interests , and Technology &amp; Computing saw distinct increases .
In the coming weeks , PubMatic will continue to watch ad spend trends across all industries and update these numbers accordingly .
