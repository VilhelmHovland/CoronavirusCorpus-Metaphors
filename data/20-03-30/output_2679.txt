Two residents of Manitoulin District test positive for COVID-19  Public Health Sudbury and District ( PHSD ) has confirmed the area 's eighth and ninth case of COVID-19 to be two residents of the Manitoulin District who recently returned from an Australian cruise .
The two individuals , one man and one female both in their 50s , were passengers of the Ruby Princess Cruise that departed Sydney , Australia on March 8 and returned to dock on March 19 .
Following the cruise , PHSD said the pair flew from Sydney to the San Francisco Airport on Quantas Airline flight QF73 .
Passengers in rows 40-44 and flight crew that may have served these rows are considered close contacts of the confirmed cases .
From San Francisco , the two flew into the Toronto International Airport on Air Canada flight AC742 .
Passengers in rows 17-21 and flight crew that may have served these rows are considered close contacts of the confirmed cases .
The individuals in question have been since been in self-isolation , said PHSD in a press release .
