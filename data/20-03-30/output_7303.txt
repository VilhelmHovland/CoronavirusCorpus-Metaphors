What does enforcement of the stay-at-home order look like in St .
Cloud ?  ST .
CLOUD -- While the St .
Cloud Police Department has changed some of its operations to cope with COVID-19 , the department is still offering the same level of service .
The department will not be stopping people just because they are outside during Gov .
Tim Walz 's stay-at-home order , which took effect Friday , St .
@ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ Kleis ' daily update .
Blair Anderson ( Photo : Courtesy of city of St .
Cloud )  The police department prides itself on " serving our community with compassion , " Anderson said , and the pandemic will not change that .
Anderson said in his observation , even before the stay-at-home order , people were listening to officials ' messages and being compliant with social distancing .
Those who need to leave the house , for activities such getting essential supplies or going to work as an essential employee , do not need a special letter or other documentation , according to Anderson .
And while the department has changed some aspects of its operations , such as medical calls , to " keep more healthy officers on the bench by limiting exposure , " Anderson said , it will continue to offer the same level of service .
" There wo n't be a drop off in service , " Anderson said .
