Coronavirus : Chart launches critical care webpage  Chart Industries has increased production in its global facilities that manufacture the company 's critical care products being used in the fight against coronavirus .
In order to facilitate access to these vital products , including liquid oxygen storage and delivery solutions for medical institutions of all sizes , and cryogenic freezers and dewars for tissue and vaccine storage , Chart has launched a critical care webpage .
Anyone who has a new requirement , wishes to expand capacity or requires spare parts for a Chart system already in use , can contact Chart through the new webpage .
In the US , Chart 's Cryo-Lease division , located in New Hampshire , currently has a limited number of oxygen trailers for lease .
More details on these mobile units are available via Chart 's customer service .
In response to the current crisis , Chart is repurposing storage tanks , utilising inventory and working with suppliers across the supply chain to reduce the lead-time on mobile oxygen units to a few weeks .
