RIO DE JANEIRO , BRAZIL - At least 70 percent of Brazilians need to be immunized before we can start talking about an end to the Covid-19 epidemic in the country .
Reaching this percentage will only be feasible after the majority of the population has been infected and cured .
The alternative is to have a vaccine , which will almost certainly not reach the market before the end of the year .
To anticipate how or when we will reach this level is an exercise in futurology .
There is no precedent in recent human history for the current crisis , thus , one can not look .
