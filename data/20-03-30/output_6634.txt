Several GE Aviation Madisonville workers test positive for COVID-19  MADISONVILLE , Ky .
( WFIE ) - A General Electric spokesperson confirms there are now several employees at the GE Aviation plant in Madisonville who have tested positive for COVID-19 .
Read the full statement below :  " GE Aviation is supporting several employees who work at our Madisonville , KY , facility who tested positive for COVID-19 .
GE 's number one priority is the health and safety of our employees .
We are partnering with public health officials and have completed contact tracing and notification of impacted people at our site .
We continue to take all necessary precautions and prioritize safety .
" -- GE spokesperson  Hopkins County Public Health Director Denise Beach confirmed there are now 17 confirmed cases of COVID-19 in the county .
