DUBAI , March 30 ( Reuters ) - Iran 's death toll from the new coronavirus has increased to 2,757 with 117 new deaths in the past 24 hours , a health ministry spokesman told state TV on Monday , adding that the total number of cases has climbed to 41,495 .
" In the past 24 hours we had 117 new deaths and 3,186 new confirmed cases of people infected with the coronavirus , " Kianush Jahanpur told state TV , calling on Iranians to stay at home .
