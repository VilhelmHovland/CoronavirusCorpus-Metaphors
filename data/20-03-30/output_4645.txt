UpdatedUpdated 18 hours ago  Share  Spain 's total number of coronavirus cases has risen to 85,195 - up from 78,797 the day before - surpassing the total number of infections reported in epicentre China .
The country also confirmed another 812 deaths in 24 hours from the coronavirus on Monday , bringing the total number of deaths to 7,340 , according to health ministry figures .
It is the first decline in the number of deaths in a 24-hour period since Thursday in Spain , which has the world 's second most deadly outbreak after Italy .
