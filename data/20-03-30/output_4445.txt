The Department of Employment and Labour 's labour centres in all nine provinces will remain closed during the 21-day lockdown .
The department appeals to its clients to stop visiting these centres , as there will be no-one to assist them during this period .
In a statement , the department emphasised that members of the public are requested to access the Department of Employment and Labour 's online services ( www .
