( MGN )  By Scott Bauer&amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp  Posted : Mon 12:53 PM , Mar 30 , 2020  ( AP ) -- Preparations for Wisconsin 's presidential primary and spring election that 's just a week away on April 7 continue , even in the face of a growing number of COVID-19 cases statewide and lawsuits seeking a delay and other changes to how the election is run .
Monday was the deadline for voters to register online or by mail to cast an absentee ballot .
Once registered , they had until Thursday to request an absentee ballot .
There remains a pending lawsuit in federal court that seeks to postpone the election , move to a mail-in voting only and make a number of other changes to facilitate more ballots being cast .
