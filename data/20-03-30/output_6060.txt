Army troops assist packing of relief assistance  BACOLOD .
Soldiers of the 303rd Brigade Philippine Army based in Barangay Minoyan , Murcia assist members of the Negros Occidental Inter-Agency Task Force on Covid-19 in packing relief goods Sunday , March 29 , 2020 .
( Contributed Photo )  PERSONNEL of the 303rd Brigade Philippine Army based in Barangay Minoyan , Murcia assisted other members of the Inter- Agency Task Force on Covid-19 in Negros Occidental .
The assistance is being prepared in preparation for the implementation of the enhanced community quarantine ( ECQ ) in the whole province starting March 30 until April 14 .
A total of 180 sacks of assorted food packs of relief goods will be distributed to the affected families .
Only one member of the family will be allowed to go out and buy food , medicine and other essentials for the family during the duration of the ECQ .
