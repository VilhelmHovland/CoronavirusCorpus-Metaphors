' Staggering ' : New York virus death toll rises above 1,200  ALBANY , N .
( WCAX ) New York remains the epicenter of the coronavirus pandemic with a big spike in deaths over the past 24 hours .
Across the state , New York has 66,497 people who have tested positive for COVID-19 .
Some 9,517 of those people are currently hospitalized .
And New York now has 1,218 deaths-- nearly half of all the deaths in the country so far .
Only one county in the state does not have a COVID-19 case .
