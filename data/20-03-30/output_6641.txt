( RTTNews ) - Provided a status update related to the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) pandemic impact , Kraton Corp .
( KRA ) announced Monday that COVID-19 has had a limited impact on its business and results of operations as its plants have continued to operate at normal capacities , and its supply chain remains intact , with adequate availability of key raw materials .
The company continues to strengthen its balance sheet , most recently through the March 6 , 2020 sale of its Cariflex business for $530 million , and the subsequent reduction in amounts outstanding under its senior secured term loan facility .
With the sale , the company expects to reduce consolidated net debt by approximately $480 million by the end of the first quarter of 2020 .
The company said it has no scheduled maturities until 2025 , with the exception of the currently largely undrawn $250 million ABL facility .
For the first quarter of 2020 , the company expects to report adjusted EBITDA exceeding the current consensus estimate of approximately $46 million .
