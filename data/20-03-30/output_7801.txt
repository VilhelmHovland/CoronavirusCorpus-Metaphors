Tokyo lockdown would have ' huge ' impact on economy : Nishimura  TOKYO ( Reuters ) - A lockdown of major Japanese cities like Tokyo and Osaka to halt the spread of the coronavirus would have a " huge " impact on the economy , Yasutoshi Nishimura , the country 's economy minister , said on Tuesday .
Nishimura also said Japan was not a situation now where it needed to declare a state of emergency and the government would consider if it needed to so " comprehensively " .
