Florida pastor arrested after holding packed church service amid COVID-19 stay-at-home order  People across the country voiced outrage online after seeing streaming video from inside The River at Tampa Bay Church Sunday morning .
The stream showed dozens of people packed inside the church , standing shoulder-to-shoulder .
Before evening services , the sheriff 's office sent deputies to the church , and even put up a sign asking people to follow social distancing guidelines .
A legal analyst says the sheriff could arrest the church 's pastor .
" He holds a service and he has a meeting of any kind where people are listening to him , then he is responsible for them being there .
They absolutely can arrest him , " the analyst said .
