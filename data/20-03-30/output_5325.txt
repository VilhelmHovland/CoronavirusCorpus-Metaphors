The conditions , known medically as anosmia and dysgeusia , respectively , are " significant symptoms " associated with the pandemic , the American Academy of Otolaryngology , or head and neck surgery , said on March 22 .
The group , based in Alexandria , Virginia , proposes that these symptoms be added to the list of screening tools for possible Covid-19 infection .
