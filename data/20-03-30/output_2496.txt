Buhari 's lockdown of Lagos , others illegal -- Adegboruwa  A Lagos based lawyer , Ebun Olu-Adegboruwa ( SAN ) , has described as illegal President Muhammadu Buhari 's order to lockdown Abuja , Lagos and Ogun states .
Buhari had during his national broadcast on Sunday directed for the lockdown of the Federal Capital Territory ( FCT ) Abuja , and the two states over the Covid-19 pandemic .
But in a statement he made available to newsmen , Adegboruwa argued that Buhari has no powers to restrict the movement of persons without recourse to the National Assembly .
" The President has not invoked his powers under the Constitution to declare any state of emergency , which must be approved by the National Assembly .
" We are running a constitutional democracy and it is illegal for the President to take over the affairs of any State of the Federation without the express consent of the people of that State through their elected representatives .
" It is only the Governor of the State through the House of Assembly of the State that can make any declaration concerning the people @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ not cite any law that he relied upon for his declaration .
" I support every measure taken to contain the Coronavirus pandemic , but such must be in accordance with the law .
If we allow this to stay , then tomorrow the President may just impose a total restriction on all states of the federation for whatever reason .
" I humbly urge the President to reverse the restrictions and work with the States concerned in accordance with the laws of our land .
" Also , there must be measures put in place to address the financial consequences of the proposed shutdown , especially for small scale businesses , those who depend on daily earnings and the needy and vulnerable members of the societies to be affected , " he said .
