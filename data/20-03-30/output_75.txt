Do n't politicize distribution of essential goods : Sajith  Samagi Jana Balawegaya ( SJB ) Leader Sajith Premadasa yesterday urged the government not to politicize the distribution of essential goods to people .
" We have been told that efforts are being made to fulfil political aspirations when essential goods are distributed to people by the government .
This should not be the case , " Mr .
Premadasa said in a statement .
He also urged the government to distribute essentials free of charge and to avoid scarcity of essential food items .
" We also urge the government to bring down equipment which is capable of diagnosing COVID-19 and to take special care in ensuring the safety of health care personnel , " Mr .
Premadasa added .
Meanwhile , a joint statement issued by the leaders of the SJB including Patali Champika Ranawaka , Rishad Bathiudeen , P Thigambaram , Mano Ganesan , Rajitha Senaratne , Rauff Hakeem and Kumara Welgama said the government should not spend funds illegally .
