WUHAN , China -- Shopkeepers in the city at the centre of the virus outbreak in China were reopening today but customers were scarce after authorities lifted more of the anti-virus controls that kept tens of millions of people at home for two months .
Many shops have imposed limits on how many people can enter .
Shopkeepers have also set up dispensers for hand sanitizer and are checking customers for signs of fever .
Meanwhile Wuhan 's bus and subway service has resumed , easing curbs that cut most access to the city of 11 million people on Jan .
23 as China fought the coronavirus .
