Two new cases of coronavirus COVID-19 confirmed in Ector County , total for county now at seven  Posted : Mon 2:51 PM , Mar 30 , 2020&amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp  Updated : Mon 5:17 PM , Mar 30 , 2020  ECTOR COUNTY , Tx .
( KOSA ) -- The Ector County Health Department has announced two new confirmed cases of coronavirus COVID-19 .
These two new cases bring the total number of cases for the county to seven .
According to a release , the first patient is a woman in her 30s .
This case is believed to have been caused by community spread but health officials are continuing to investigate .
The woman is now being evaluated by medical professionals .
The second patient is a man in his 40s who is believed to have contracted the virus while traveling .
He is now under self-quarantine at home .
Officials will be discussing these two new cases at a news conference at 4 p .
on Monday .
