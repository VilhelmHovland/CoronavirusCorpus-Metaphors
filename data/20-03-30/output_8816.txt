Houston County Sheriff 's Office building closed to the public because of COVID-19  The Houston County Sheriff 's Office is currently located on North Oates Street in downtown Dothan .
( Source : WTVY )  By Press **25;1302;TOOLONG  Posted : Mon 4:02 AM , Mar 30 , 2020  Houston County , Ala .
( PR ) -- With the Coronavirus still spreading and for the safety and concern for the citizens of Houston County and the employee 's of Houston County Sheriff 's Office , starting Monday March 30 the Houston County Sheriff 's Office building will be closed to the public .
Houston County Sheriff 's Office will continue to serve the citizens as HCSO has Patrol Units on duty 24 hours a day seven days a week .
