( photo credit : WANA NEWS AGENCY/REUTERS )  BERLIN -- The Islamic Republic of Iran 's health minister , Saeid Namaki , announced on Monday that the country is not experiencing a shortage of special drugs in the fight against the coronavirus .
Namaki said that " Although it is hard to fight the coronavirus under sanctions , since the beginning of the outbreak we have not faced a shortage of special drugs needed to treat this disease .
"  The pro-Islamic Republic network Tasnim News first reported Namaki 's comments , delivering a blow to advocates of lifting US sanctions against Iran 's regime for medical reasons .
