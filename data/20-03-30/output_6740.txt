( FOX Image )  BREVARD COUNTY , FLORIDA ( FOX 35 ) -- Carnival Cruise Line announced on Monday that as coronavirus continues to impact global health and commerce , they have extended their pause in operations until May 11 .
The original suspension was through April 11 , but with that date quickly approaching and positive cases still rising , the extended suspension was announced .
They said that all impacted travelers have received an email from either Carnival directly or Travel Advisor .
Directions to claim your cancelation offer or refund is in the email .
The quickest way to make this claim is by using the link in the email , they added .
