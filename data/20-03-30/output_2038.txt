US lab unveils portable 5-minute Covid-19 test  The test , which is the size of a small toaster and uses molecular technology  A US-based lab has unveiled a portable test that can tell if someone has Covid-19 in as little as five minutes , it said in a statement Friday .
Abbott Laboratories said the US Food and Drug Administration ( FDA ) had given it emergency authorization to begin making the test available to healthcare providers as early as next week .
The test , which is the size of a small toaster and uses molecular technology , also shows negative results within 13 minutes , the company said in a press statement .
" The Covid-19 pandemic will be fought on multiple fronts , and a portable molecular test that offers results in minutes adds to the broad range of diagnostic solutions needed to combat this virus , " said Abbott president and chief operating officer Robert Ford .
