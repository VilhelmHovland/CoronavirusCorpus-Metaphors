Coronavirus : 4 new cases , including two-year-old reported from Noida ; state tally at 75  Four fresh cases , including a two-year old , of Sars-Cov-2 have been reported from Uttar Pradesh 's Gautam Buddh Nagar on Monday taking the total number of cases in the district to 36 , official siad .
Three out of the four patients are of a family .
Those tested positive include the 37-year-old wife , 25-year-old sister and two-year old son of a man from Accheja village in Dadri area who was diagnose with the infection on 28th March , Health Department officials said .
Another positive case has surfaced from Ghodi Baccheda village in Greater Noida and the patient is a 21-year-old man .
Official said .
The state of Uttar Pradesh has thus far reported 75 cases , while the number of Sars-Cov-2 cases mounted to 1,071 in India on Monday , and the death toll climbed to 29 , as maintained by the Ministry of Health .
