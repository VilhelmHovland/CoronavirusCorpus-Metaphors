Senators donate 50% of their salaries to fight Coronavirus  Nigerian senators have announced their decision to donate 50 per cent of their salaries to fight the coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) pandemic .
This was disclosed by the acting spokesman of the Senate , Senator Godiya Akwashiki in a statement released on Monday .
It comes days after ministers donated 50 per cent of their salaries to assist the country in her fight against the dreaded disease .
The senators ' statement read :  " After due consultations following a keen review of the national efforts to contain the Coronavirus pandemic in Nigeria , the Senate wishes to announce that from March 2020 , Distinguished Senators will be donating 50 per cent of their salaries to these efforts to stop the spread of the disease , otherwise known as COVID-19 , in our country .
" This monthly contribution from the Upper Legislative Chamber will be sustained until Nigeria is declared safe from the ravages of this deadly disease .
