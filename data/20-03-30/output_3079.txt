COVID-19 : NACA charges HIV patients to follow directives of experts  The National Agency for the Control of AIDS ( NACA ) has charged people living with HIV to take all necessary precautions given by World Health Organisation ( WHO ) and National Centre for Disease Control ( NCDC ) in staying safe from contracting COVID-19 .
The Director General of NACA Gambo Gumel Aliyu said " COVID-19 is a serious global respiratory pandemic .
People with weak body defence systems suffer serious consequences " .
In a statement issued and signed by the NACA DG , it charged those living with HIV to observe their body system to know when they develop the symptoms of COVID-19 so that early treatment could be administered .
He also told them to stay at home , observe handwashing and personal hygiene and always maintain social distance .
" People living with HIV must continue to take all the necessary precautions given by WHO and NCDC with special emphasis on adherence to ARV medications , minimizing exposure to COVID by staying at home , hand washing and personal hygiene , and social distancing .
