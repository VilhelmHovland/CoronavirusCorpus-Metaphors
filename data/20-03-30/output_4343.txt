Life Credit Union closes Naas town centre office services due to Covid-19 - other locations still open  Services available at Monread , Newbridge and Maynooth  Reporter :  Paul O'Meara  30 Mar 2020  Email :  paul@leinsterleader .
0  Life Credit Union 's premises on Naas ( file photo )  Life Credit Union has shut its Naas town centre office to the public with immediate effect - because of Covid-19 .
However the Monread office will remain open , with some services also available in Maynooth and Newbridge .
" The situation is changing almost daily and we are continuously reviewing our situation , with the safety of our members and staff paramount .
Whilst we will endeavour to give you notice of changes to our member service , there may be some situations when we need to make last minute decisions .
We ask you to bear with us and can assure you that we are working hard to maintain our services to you , " Life CU said in a statement .
