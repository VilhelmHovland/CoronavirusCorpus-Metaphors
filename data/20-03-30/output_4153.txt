A NEW e-learning project supported by Limerick City and County Council is helping the people of Limerick to identify and record the bumblebees they come across .
' What 's The Buzz ' was developed by local bee expert Liz Gabbett in collaboration with the National Biodiversity Database Centre .
Limerick City and County Council has funded the completion of the project to mark their designation as European Green Leaf City 2020 .

Ireland has 21 species of bumblebees and they are the most abundant and widespread wild pollinator with 80 percent of wild bees being a bumblebee .
