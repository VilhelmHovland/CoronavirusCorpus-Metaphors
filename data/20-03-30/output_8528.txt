Share this :  On Sunday evening , the Santa Barbara County Sheriff 's Department issued a press release stating that two deputies have tested positive for COVID-19 and are currently self-isolating at home .
One of the deputies , who last worked patrol in Santa Maria on Saturday , March 21 , began displaying symptoms the following day .
After being tested that Monday , the deputy began self-isolating and was notified on Friday they 'd tested positive for COVID-19 .
That same Friday , their partner from a previous patrol began displaying symptoms , was tested , and began self-isolating as well .
The following day , the second deputy received word they had also tested positive .
According to the press release , no additional coworkers or members of the public the two deputies had contact with have been instructed to self-isolate .
