Tokyo Olympic Games confirmed for 23 July to 8 August 2021  The Tokyo 2020 Olympic Games are set to start on the 23 July 2021 and run until the 8 August 2021 .
The announcement was made following an IOC Executive Board meeting today .
The Opening Ceremony of the XXXII Olympic Games had been scheduled for the 24 July this year and were forced to be postponed due to the ongoing Covid-19 pandemic .
The delay of one year was agreed by the board following discussions with the Tokyo 2020 organising committee and the International Federations .
The World Athletics Championships scheduled for Oregon , USA , in 2021 , will now move back a year to 2022 to facilitate the Tokyo Olympic Games next year .
