About the DPX Platform  DPX is the Company 's proprietary lipid-based delivery platform with no aqueous component in the final formulation .
The DPX platform can be formulated with peptide antigens .
Its unique " no release " mechanism of action allows antigen presenting cells ( APCs ) to be attracted to the injection site , facilitating a robust and sustained immune response within lymph nodes .
Fully synthetic , easy to manufacture ; each product is stored in dry form and reconstituted in lipids for injection , providing an extended shelf life and simple handling and administration in the clinic .
