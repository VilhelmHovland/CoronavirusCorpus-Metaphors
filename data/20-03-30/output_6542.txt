Gulf Power has announced its plan to significantly lower bills for customers amid the ongoing coronavirus ( COVID-19 ) pandemic .
Subject to Florida Public Service Commission approval , beginning May 1 , Gulf Power plans to issue a one-time decrease of approximately 40% for the typical residential customer bill as a result of lower fuel costs .
" At Gulf Power , we understand how critical it is -- particularly at a time like now -- for you to have the reliable service that you count on , each and every day .
We also understand that , at this moment , savings on your bill can be especially valuable , " said Gulf Power President Marlene Santos .
" While traditionally these types of savings are spread out , we know that many people in our communities are struggling right now and every penny counts .
We want to do what we can to keep as much money as possible in our customers ' pockets , and we believe this is the best way to provide customers with much-needed money as we all navigate through this difficult and unsettling time together .
