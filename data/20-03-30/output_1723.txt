The 55-year-old is one of three front-line NHS medics to have died after contracting Covid-19 .
It is understood he was being treated in Glenfield 's ECMO unit .
He worked at the Queen 's Hospital in Burton and fellow medics said he was known for his dedication and commitment to patients .
The University Hospitals of Derby and Burton NHS Foundation Trust has asked for a minutes silence to be observed across its site at 1pm .
The doctor 's son Ashraf El-Hawrani , 18 , yesterday paid an emotional tribute to his father saying : " Most of my dad 's time was dedicated towards his family , and the rest of that time was dedicated towards his profession .
" He taught me the significance of respect and equality .
He also stressed the importance of not worrying about the things I can not control , which he displayed to me right up until the end of his life .
