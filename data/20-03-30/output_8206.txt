Harrisburg , PA- March 20 , 2020  New data from the state shows that more than half of Pennsylvania 's confirmed coronavirus patients are under 50 years old , while seniors make up less than a quarter of cases .
Information provided @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ Health shows 12% of patients are between 19 and 24 , while another 39% are between 25 and 49 .
Seniors aged 65 or older , one of the groups most susceptible to serious complications , make up 18% of cases .
Children 18 and under make up the smallest group , at roughly 3% .
As of Thursday , there were 1,687 confirmed cases in 48 of Pennsylvania 's 67 counties .
The number of new cases being reported each day is rising exponentially , with 560 additional cases reported between Wednesday and Thursday .
Sixteen people have died statewide .
