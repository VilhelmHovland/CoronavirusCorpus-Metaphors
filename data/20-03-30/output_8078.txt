No more St .
Cloud home for home brewing supplies ; Hop Shop closes  ST .
CLOUD -- The Hop Shop , a store supplying St .
Cloud-area home brewers and winemakers , has closed after a little over three years in operation and almost exactly one year in its downtown location .

Hall said The Hop Shop paid for itself but he was essentially working there for free -- in addition to working another full-time job .
