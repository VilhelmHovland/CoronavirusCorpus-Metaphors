Share  In a bid to fight the coronavirus outbreak in the country , Northern Railway has successfully made the first prototype of hospital isolation coach for the treatment of corona patients by converting non-air-conditioned train coaches .
In order to construct an isolation cabin prototype for the patients , the middle berth has been removed from one side of the coach while all three berths have been removed in front of the patient berth , a senior official said , adding that all ladders for climbing up the berths have also been removed .
The bathrooms , aisle areas and other areas have also been modified to prepare the isolation coach .
Each railway zone would manufacture a rake with 10 coaches every week after once the best practices were finalised in the next few days , it said in a statement .
" Then we will serve the hinterlands or whichever region needs the coaches , " Northern Railway Spokesperson Deepak Kumar said .
