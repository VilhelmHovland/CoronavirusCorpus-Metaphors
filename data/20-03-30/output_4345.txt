Over the weekend  According to Gardai there are still some offenders out there -- offences detected varied from no insurance -Tax -- NCT -- Provisional Licence Driver unaccompanied -- parking in a disabled parking bay -- driving whilst holding a Mobile Phone - Dangerous driving ( 174kmh on the M4 ) and speeding ( Several Motorists Detected Driving at Excessive Speed on M4 and M7 ) .
Fines were issued in some matters and court proceeding in other matters .
Gardai are urging people to please comply with Government regulations and stay at home .
