There are now 36 confirmed cases of COVID-19 in Jamaica .
Additional results received at 9:00 p .
from the National Influenza Centre , showed that of the five pending results from yesterday , three samples tested negative , while two were positive for COVID-19 .
The two new cases are : * a 45 year old female , a healthcare worker , who was a contact of a confirmed case and * a 54 year old female from St .
Mary .
Her mode of transmission is under investigation .
As of this evening , the healthcare worker is well and in good spirit .
The Ministry would like to advise the public that there is no other healthcare worker at present , who is positive for COVID-19 .
There are now twenty-three ( 23 ) imported cases , eleven ( 11 ) import-related and two ( 2 ) cases are under investigation .
