Eight patients diagnosed with COVID-19 in Ireland have died .
Six deaths were located in the east , one in the south and one in the west of the country .
The Health Protection Surveillance Centre said the patients included five females and three males .
The median age of today 's reported deaths is 86 .
Six patients were reported as having underlying health conditions There have now been 54 COVID-19 related deaths in Ireland .
Meath has 47 confirmed cases of Covid-19 as of Monday 30th March with 2,910 confirmed cases of COVID-19 in Ireland .
The HSE is now working to identify any contacts the patients may have had to provide them with information and advice to prevent further spread .
