Macy 's to furlough most of its 130K workers amid coronavirus shutdown  Updated Mar 30 , 2020 ; Posted Mar 30 , 2020  Macy 's job cuts also include its Bloomingdale 's and Blue Mercury brands , as the company is ' moving to the absolute minimum workforce needed to maintain basic operations ' during the coronavirus shutdown .
( Frank Franklin II AP Photo )  Macy 's announced on Monday that the majority of its workers will be furloughed as a result of temporary store closures due to the coronavirus pandemic .
The furloughs begin this week and also include the Bloomingdale 's and Blue Mercury brands , as the company is " moving to the absolute minimum workforce needed to maintain basic operations , " the statement said .
Macy 's digital business , supporting distribution centers and call centers will experience fewer furloughs so the company can continue to serve customers online , the statement said .
Furloughed employees who are enrolled in health benefits will continue to receive coverage , and Macy 's says it plans to bring back employees " on a staggered basis as business resumes @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ , we have lost the majority of our sales due to the store closures , " Macy 's said in the statement .
" We 've already taken measures to maintain financial flexibility , including suspending the dividend , drawing down our line of credit , freezing both hiring and spending , stopping capital spend , reducing receipts , cancelling some orders and extending payment terms , and we are evaluating all other financing options .
"  All stores will remain closed until Macy 's has a " clear line of sight on when it is safe to reopen , " the statement said .
