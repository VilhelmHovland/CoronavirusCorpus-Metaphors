LINCOLN , Neb .

Pete Ricketts and Nebraska Commissioner of Labor John Albin announced its plan to get unemployed people checks during this pandemic Monday .
Albin said this is a part of the federal CARES Act .
Part of this includes a new portion called the Pandemic Unemployment Assistance .
People are allowed to apply for this if they work as a hairstylist , barber or are self-employed .
Those unable to collect these benefits include those who can work from home or can work .
