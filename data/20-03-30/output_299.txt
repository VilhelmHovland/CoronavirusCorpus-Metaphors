An Arizona man has died after ingesting chloroquine phosphate -- believing it would protect him from becoming infected with the coronavirus .
The man 's wife also ingested the substance and is under critical care .
The toxic ingredient they consumed was not the medication form of chloroquine , used to treat malaria in humans .
Instead , it was an ingredient listed on a parasite treatment for fish .
The name " chloroquine " resonated with the man 's wife , who asked that her name not be used to protect the family 's privacy .
