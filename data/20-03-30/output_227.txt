Coronavirus : New case in Nelson , now 20 cases in top of the south  A new case of coronavirus has been confirmed in Nelson , while no new cases have been reported in Marlborough .
On Tuesday , Director-General of Health Ashley Bloomfield announced 58 new cases of Covid-19 , bringing the nation 's total of confirmed and probable cases to 647 .
There have been no additional deaths since a West Coast woman with the virus died on Sunday .
A woman in her 20s who recently returned to Nelson from Australia was confirmed as having coronavirus .
That brings the total number of confirmed cases in Nelson to 11 , as well as one probable case .
In Marlborough , there are nine confirmed cases and one probable case .
Across the top of the south there are 20 confirmed and two probable cases .
