Amidst the coronavirus pandemic , the South film industry has come forward to lend its support to the state and central government .
Not only are the big names urging people to stay indoors , but they have also donated money to relief funds working to support the country 's health care system .
Leading the pack is Bahubali actor Prabhas , who contributed Rs 3 crore to the Prime Minister Relief Fund and Rs 50 lakh each to CM relief funds of Andhra Pradesh and Telangana .
The actor recently returned from Georgia after the shoot of his next period romance with Pooja Hegde was halted due to coronavirus scare .
Telugu actor-politician Pawan Kalyan has donated Rs 1 crore to the PM 's relief fund and Rs 50 lakh each to both the Andhra Pradesh and Telangana CM relief funds .
