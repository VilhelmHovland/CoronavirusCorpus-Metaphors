Nine suspends magazines as pandemic impacts advertising business  Nine is suspending a number of print sections to deal with the impact the coronavirus pandemic is having on its advertising revenue .
Non-weekly magazines such as Boss , Sophisticated Traveller , Luxury , Good Food Magazine and Executive Style will be paused , with some content to be used across other magazines and newspapers at Nine .
The Traveller liftout , Domain magazines and The Shortlist event guide are also impacted .
Nine says impacted staff will be redeployed across its business .
" Like most businesses , Nine is examining its business in light of the COVID-19 outbreak and its impact upon the advertising market and is taking a number of steps including the suspension of certain print sections , " a Nine spokesperson says .
" These temporary measures will be continually reviewed and the sections will be reinstated as conditions improve .
" Impacted staff will be redeployed as appropriate .
