Wildwood man taken to hospital after injuring arm while breaking window  March 30 , 2020  Justin Jernigan  A Wildwood man was taken to UF Health Leesburg Hospital after injuring his arm while breaking a window .
Justin Wayne Jernigan , 36 , allegedly woke a sleeping man when he broke his window at about 4 a .
Sunday , according to an arrest report from the Sumter County Sheriff 's office .
Jernigan stuck his head through the broken window and told the man , " I 'm coming to get you .
"  Jernigan then asked the man to give him a ride to the hospital for the treatment of his arm , which was " heavily bleeding .
" He took Jernigan to the hospital and then signed an intent to prosecute after contacting law enforcement .
Jernigan was arrested on a charge of criminal mischief .
He was booked at the Sumter County Detention Center and released after posting $1,000 bond .
