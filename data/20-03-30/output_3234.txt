Govs hold teleconference , react to lockdown of states  The Nigeria Governors ' Forum ( NGF ) on Sunday pledged commitment to ensuring seamless movement of essential commodities throughout the country during the period of lockdown declared in some states .
The forum made the pledge in a communiqu ? issued after its first teleconference meeting held on Sunday and signed by Gov .
Kayode Fayomi of Ekiti who is the NGF Chairman .
The communiqu ? stated that the governors through the teleconference meeting deliberated on the COVID-19 pandemic in the country .
