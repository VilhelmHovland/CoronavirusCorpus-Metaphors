Pasig City hospitals on the brink of saturation point : Vico Sotto appeals for more doctors , nurses  Pasig City Mayor Vico Sotto is calling for additional health professionals and workers to join the fight against the coronavirus outbreak .
In his latest Twitter post , Sotto made the appeal for more doctors and nurses as the hospitals in Pasig City are on the brink of saturation point .
He would welcome any doctors and nurses willing to work full or part time at the city 's health facilities .
