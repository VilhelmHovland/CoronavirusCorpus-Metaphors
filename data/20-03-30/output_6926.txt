( Photo : Warchi , Getty Images/iStockphoto )  Locally owned businesses are facing difficult times as the coronavirus requires massive closures across Great Falls and Montana .
To help , the Great Falls Chamber of Commerce has released a small business checklist and a list of resources @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @  According to the Chamber , the Coronavirus Aid , Relief and Economic Security ( CARES ) Act has allocated $350 billion to help keep workers at small businesses employed during the pandemic and economic downturn .
The initiative provides federally guaranteed loans that may be forgiven if borrowers maintain their payrolls during the crisis or restore their payrolls afterward .
As more details about the act are released , the U .
Chamber of Commerce has issued a guide to help eligible business owners prepare to file for a loan .
Am I eligible ?  Under the act , you are considered eligible if you are a small business with fewer than 500 employees or otherwise meet the Small Business Administration 's size standard .
A 501(c) ( 3 ) with fewer than 500 employees is also eligible .
As an individual , you qualify if you operate as a sole proprietor , an independent contractor or are self-employed and regularly carry on any trade or business .
