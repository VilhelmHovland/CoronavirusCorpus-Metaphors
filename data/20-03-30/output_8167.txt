COVID-19 testing sites charging hundreds to test  Some will do anything to be tested for COVID-19 , but one local drive-thru location is offering tests for $200 each .
( WJHG/WECP )  PANAMA CITY , Fla .
( WJHG/WECP ) - Some people will do anything to be tested for COVID-19 , even if it means paying hundreds of dollars , but officials with the Department of Health say that 's not necessary when there are other free testing opportunities .
However , one organization advertised a drive-thru testing site in Panama City charging $200 for a COVID-19 test .
Local health department officials say Monday was the first time the organization in charge of the testing reached out to the DOH .
Heather Kretzer , public information officer for the Department of Health in Bay County said , " The news release that was sent by the Governor on Friday , and also his news conference Friday , he did reference a hospital in Miami-Dade that was charging I believe $150 for the test and he had asked that the state attorney general , Ashley Moody , look into that and indicated @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ the organization running the drive-thru testing wanted to meet the need of those who want to get tested but do n't meet the CDC criteria .
But DOH officials say if you are interested in being tested for free , you can call your local department of health office .
