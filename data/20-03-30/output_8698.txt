CCP Virus Patients Cremated Within an Hour After Death  A patient died within half an hour of being admitted to the hospital and cremated within an hour .
A Wuhan resident tells us the tragedy he witnessed with his own mother 's death .
Since March 23 , Wuhan funeral homes have been stacked with thousands of urns and crowds of people , waiting to pick up the ashes of their relatives who died during the CCP virus outbreak .
NTD refers to the novel coronavirus , which causes the disease COVID-19 , as the CCP virus because the Chinese Communist Party 's coverup and mismanagement allowed the virus to spread throughout China and create a global pandemic .
