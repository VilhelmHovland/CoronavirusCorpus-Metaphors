EasyJet grounds its entire fleet due to pandemic  Anthony O .
Goriainoff  EasyJet PLC said on Monday that it was grounding its entire aircraft fleet due to the measures against the coronavirus pandemic imposed by governments across Europe .
The budget airline said it will continue working with government bodies to operate additional rescue flights as requested .
The company said there can be no certainty as to when it will restart commercial flights .
It said it was taking measures to remove cost and noncritical expenditure at every level to mitigate the hit from the coronavirus .
