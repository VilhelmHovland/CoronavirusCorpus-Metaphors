FOND DU LAC - City and county law enforcement were kept busy over the weekend investigating reports of potential violations of state-issued orders that limit gatherings of more than 10 people and shuttered numerous , non-essential businesses to curtail the spread of COVID-19 .
In one instance , an establishment on North Main Street in Fond du Lac hosted a birthday party .

In another case , customers were picking up food orders from a local restaurant on Friday night and not adhering to the social distancing rules .
One customer , who was seated in a back room , reported she had just ordered food and the other stated she was there for over an hour , ate food and chatted with friends , the log entry states .
The owner , as well as the customer , were advised of charges that could be issued .
As of Friday , all bars were closed and restaurants were to close their dining rooms and only offer takeout to those who order over the phone or online before coming to pick up their orders , said Fond du Lac County Health Officer Kim Mueller .
Customers can not order in-person or wait inside for their orders to be made .
Restaurants can remain open for delivery and drive-thru orders only .
