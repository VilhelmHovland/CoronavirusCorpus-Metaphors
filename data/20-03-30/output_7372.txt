Canada 's Come By Chance refinery to shut due to virus -local media  North Atlantic Refining Limited 's 130,000 barrels per day Come by Chance refinery in Newfoundland and Labrador , Canada will be shut down this week due to the coronavirus , a local media report said citing the president of the United Steelworkers Union .
March 30 ( Reuters ) - North Atlantic Refining Limited 's 130,000 barrels per day Come by Chance refinery in Newfoundland and Labrador , Canada will be shut down this week due to the coronavirus , a local media report said citing the president of the United Steelworkers Union .
Though the period of the shutdown is uncertain , it could be anywhere from 2 to 5 months , the report dated March 29 said .
