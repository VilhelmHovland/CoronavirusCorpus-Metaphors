Montrose County has three additional cases of COVID-19  MONTROSE COUNTY , Colo .
( KKCO ) As of Monday evening , March 30 , there are three additional positive cases of COVID-19 in Montrose County bringing the total to 14 .
Two patients are hospitalized , and the rest are in self-quarantine at their residences .
The three additional cases include two males and one female , all over the age of 70 .
