Queens Borough President Special Election Pushed To June  The Queens borough president special election , which was canceled due to concerns over the spread of the new coronavirus , will be in June .
Mar 30 , 2020 6:35 pm ET  ( Shutterstock )  QUEENS , NY -- The Queens borough president special election that was canceled amid growing concerns over the spread of the new coronavirus has been rescheduled for June 23 , Gov .
Andrew Cuomo said Monday .
The special election will determine who replaces Queens District Attorney Melinda Katz , the former Queens borough president , for the rest of this year .
Voters will get a choice between six candidates : City Council Member Costa Constantinides , former City Council Member Elizabeth Crowley , former NYPD sergeant Anthony Miranda , former prosecutor Jim Quinn , City Council Member Donovan Richards and businessman Dao Yin .
