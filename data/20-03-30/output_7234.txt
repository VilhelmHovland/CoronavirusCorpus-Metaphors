Klitschko : 102 coronavirus cases confirmed in Kyiv city  30 .
12:32  Ukrinform  The number of Kyiv residents infected with the coronavirus Covid-19 has grown to 102 people , according to Kyiv Mayor Vitali Klitschko .
" In the capital , the number of people infected with the coronavirus is growing rapidly .
As of this morning , there were 102 such people .
We received 20 more laboratory-confirmed coronavirus cases from the State Institution Kyiv City Center of the Health Ministry of Ukraine , " he said at a briefing on Monday , March 30 .
As reported , as of 10:00 , March 30 , Ukraine had 480 laboratory-confirmed coronavirus cases .
