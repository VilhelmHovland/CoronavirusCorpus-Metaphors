The following is a note sent Monday to Logix Federal Credit Union members by Ana E .

Those Logix team members that were in direct contact with the employee are currently at home under a 14-day self-quarantine , and they continue to be paid during this time .
We are following all local and state guidance and taking necessary measures to prevent the spread of the virus .
