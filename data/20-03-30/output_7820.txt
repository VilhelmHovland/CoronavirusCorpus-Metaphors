Virginia 's Coronavirus Cases Spike by 130 in One Day : Health Dept .
**FILE** Virginia Governor Ralph Northam ( D ) attends a rally for Virginia State Senator and candidate for the U .
House of Representatives Jennifer Wexton ( D ) at the Wexton campaign headquarters on October 30 , 2018 in Sterling , Virginia .
( Mark Wilson/Getty Images )  Virginia 's confirmed coronavirus cases surpassed 1,000 as of early Monday morning , increasing by 130 cases since Sunday , according to the state health department .
Overall , 25 Virginians have died from COVID-19 , the respiratory disease caused by the novel coronavirus , and 136 have been hospitalized .
According to the health department count released Monday , 12,038 people have been tested , more than double than the total as of Wednesday .
Ralph Northam tweeted Monday morning that he would make " a major announcement " later in the day about the state 's response to the COVID-19 pandemic .
