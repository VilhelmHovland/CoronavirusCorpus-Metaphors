Breakfast with Bongani Bingwa  The Red Cross is working with state governments , thousands of volunteers across the country , and millions across the world to respond to the COVID-19 pandemic .
To find out what this work entails and what the organisation is doing in South Africa , Azania spoke head of the Southern African Cluster of the International Federation of the Red Cross Dr Michael Charles .
Specifically for Covid you will see that it has reached over 184 countries globally .
The Red Cross movement , we are in 192 countries .
In all these countries where there is Covid , the Red Cross is present , working side by side with government and really trying to exercise its auxiliary role , working at a community level and doing things like contact tracing , screening , providing psycho-social support just to name a few .
