" We have been in constant communication with our house parents regarding the safety and well-being @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ are all healthy and doing well , despite the circumstances , " the Boyds wrote .
Matthew and Ashley Boyd established Kingdom Home in Uganda in 2018 .
( Photo : Kingdom Home )  Matt and Ashley established Kingdom Home in Uganda more than two years ago to house , raise and educate girls who were victims of or at risk of being victims of the sex trade .
There are presently some 200 children , boys and girls , plus 17 parents ( staff members ) living in four homes .
" Now more than ever is the time to look out for each other , " the Boyds wrote .
" While we practice social distancing , we still have the ability to come together in a unique way -- united as we share this common experience and together trust that the Lord will carry us through this time and make us stronger because of it .
"  By latest reports , the number of positive tests for the virus in Uganda has jumped from five to 30 in less than a week .
