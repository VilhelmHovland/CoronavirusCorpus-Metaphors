Mar 30 , 2020 ( Baystreet .
via COMTEX ) -- Abbott Laboratories ( NYSE:ABT ) reported that the U .
Food and Drug Administration has issued Emergency Use Authorization for a diagnostic test for the COVID-19 that can deliver results within minutes .
Abbott is n't the first company in the U .
to launch a point-of-care coronavirus test , but the Abbott ID NOW COVID-19 test is much quicker than the competition .
On March 22 , Danaher ( NYSE:DHR ) received a EUA for a test that can provide a result in about 45 minutes .
Sources say this is Abbott 's second EUA for a COVID-19 test this month .
About a week ago , the FDA green-lighted a test that runs on systems generally limited to laboratories and hospitals .
According to Abbott , more ID NOW machines are available to run its new COVID-19 test than any other point-of-care molecular diagnostic platform .
