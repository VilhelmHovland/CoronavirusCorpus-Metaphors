Here 's what Springwater services are available during pandemic  NEWS RELEASE TOWNSHIP OF SPRINGWATER **25;1743;TOOLONG With the Province of Ontario announcing the closure of all non-essential businesses in Ontario , The Township of Springwater has realigned its services to ensure our staff and residents remain safe and our critical services and infrastructure are maintained .
In an effort to maintain proper physical distancing and ensure the health and well-being of our employees , staff members , where possible will continue to provide services online and by telephone , while working from home .
Staff that work out of facilities ( Public Works and Recreation , Parks &amp; Facilities ) and in the public will work in smaller teams and on shifts , while practicing safe physical distancing .
Staff are available Monday to Friday from 8:30 a .
-- 4:30 p .
via email or phone .
For after-hours service please call 705-725-2715 .
