Beijing : The virus pandemic has advanced with the US and Europe reporting soaring numbers of new cases , prompting a scramble in some regions to set up additional hospital beds and replenish much-needed medical supplies .
Italy announced its biggest day-to-day increase of infections , which rose by 6600 with nearly 800 new fatalities from the virus that causes COVID-19 .
The country 's total death toll of more than 4825 has surpassed the number of deaths in China , where the first cases emerged late last year .
